💰 Three Main Profit Models

  1. Content Matrix (Traffic Farming) — AI mass-produces content distributed across multi-account networks; monetized through ad revenue
  2. Micro-SaaS (Mini Software Factory) — Identify pain point → AI rapid development → subscription billing. Find 1,000 paying users and you’re set
  3. Service Arbitrage (Middleman Model) — Take orders from traditional clients (translation, design, proposals), execute with AI, profit from the information gap

7. Risk Alert: Not All Glitters

⚠️ One-person company pitfalls to watch:

  • Content homogeneity — AI-generated content risk of platform penalties or demotion
  • Single point of failure — Solo founder bears all pressure with weak risk resistance
  • Data compliance — Privacy and security regulations can’t be ignored
  • Tool dependency — AI service price hikes or shutdowns have outsized impact
  • Recommendation: Don’t put all eggs in one basket; build backup toolchains
