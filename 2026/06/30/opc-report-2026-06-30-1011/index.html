4. Market Data: From Trend to Validation

4.1 AI Side Hustle Market Exceeds 200 Billion

The 2026 AI gig economy market has surpassed 200 billion RMB, with individual contractors accounting for over 30%. Case studies show ordinary administrative staff in second-tier cities earning 12,000 RMB/month in side income within three months using AI tools, tripling their primary salary.

4.2 Rapid Community Ecosystem Development

OPC communities in 2026 are evolving from simple co-working spaces or entrepreneurial communities into new organizational carriers connecting AI tools, individual entrepreneurs, industrial resources, and enterprise application scenarios. The value of OPC communities lies not just in “enabling one person to start a business” but in ensuring individuals no longer start businesses in isolation.

5. Technical Evolution: From Tools to Agents

  • edisonwong520/opc: Expanded to a 9-role execution system (including DEV/QA capabilities)
  • iamtouchskyer/opc: Implements 16 specialized agents (PM, Designer, Security, Devil’s Advocate, etc.) with digraph-based pipelines and code-enforced quality gates for code building, review, and evaluation

5.2 AI-Autonomous Social Networks

MoltBook has become the world’s first AI-autonomous digital public space where humans can only observe while AI autonomously posts, interacts, and forms communities and public opinion ecosystems. Within 48 hours, it attracted 100K+ agents and millions of user observations, marking AI’s transition from “tool assistance” to “autonomous agency.”

6. Entrepreneur Action Guide

6.1 Three Key Mindset Shifts

  • From “Code Writer” to “System Architect”: Your value lies in “defining boundaries” and “orchestrating logic”
  • Become an Agent Architect: Design multi-agent collaboration flows, think about task decomposition, state transitions, and human-in-the-loop nodes
  • Core Principle: 2026’s winners don’t write programs; they orchestrate intelligence

6.2 Pitfall Avoidance Guide

⚠️ Critical Reminder: Don’t pursue the “one person does everything” OPC model. The OPC worth discussing in 2026 is one where an individual learns to design task flows, then uses AI agents, automation tools, and external service networks to execute tasks. AI has, for the first time, expanded the “manageable radius” for ordinary people.

7. Future Outlook

Sam Altman’s prediction is coming true—“In the AI era, one person can potentially found a billion-dollar unicorn company.” AI entrepreneurship is transitioning from individual point attempts to a new incubation model supported by “tool capabilities, scenario validation, talent organization, and enterprise demand.”

One-person companies don’t mean fighting alone; they mean completing lighter project validation with the help of AI tools and community platforms. OPC is moving from the fringe terminology of tech circles to the center stage of business discussions.
