⚠️ Risks and Challenges

5.1 Engineering Pitfalls

For independent developers or 3-5 person small teams, “hand-crafting an Agent” seems like an accessible threshold, but this may be a high-risk trap leading to technology stack失控 (loss of control) and project failure. Small teams need to carefully evaluate their core survival space between the clear and ambiguous boundaries of standard operating procedures (SOP).

5.2 Compliance Risks

Although the OPC model is flexible, there are compliance risks in taxation, contracts, intellectual property, etc. It is recommended that OPC entrepreneurs consult professional advisors in the early stages and establish basic legal and financial frameworks.

5.3 Sustainability Challenges

The OPC model heavily relies on the founder’s personal capabilities and time investment. How to achieve sustainable business growth and avoid the founder becoming a bottleneck requires advance planning. The introduction of AI Agents can alleviate this problem to some extent, but still requires reasonable system design.
