📖 OPC Core Definition

OPC (One-Person Company) represents a major transformation in business organizational form. With AI technology, individual entrepreneurs can independently operate complete business loops—from product development, marketing, to customer service, achieving full-chain intelligent management.

🎯 Three Types of Successful OPC

  1. Technology Expert, Disruptive Advantage — Possessing core technical barriers, crushing the market with technical capabilities
  2. Niche Market, Deep Vertical — Finding small but essential market segments
  3. Service OPC, AI Efficiency — Using AI tools to significantly improve service delivery efficiency

📌 Key Insights

⚠️ OPC ≠ Working Alone

One-person companies don’t mean one person doing everything. In reality, individual entrepreneurs still need office space, computing resources, business registration, legal compliance, project connections, market feedback, peer exchange, and industry chain collaboration.

💎 Core Formula
