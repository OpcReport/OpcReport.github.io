1. OPC Model Definition & Core Features

1.1 What is OPC One-Person Company?

OPC (One-Person Company) in the 2026 context is no longer the traditional “individual business” or “freelancer”, but a brand-new business organization form:

💡 Core Formula of OPC

Carbon-based Wisdom (Human) + Silicon-based Execution (AI) = Complete Company

  • 1 Core Decision Maker: Responsible for strategy, creativity, quality control
  • N AI Digital Employees: Undertake programming, design, marketing, customer service, etc.
  • ∞ Global Collaboration Network: Expand capability boundaries through outsourcing platforms, API services, cloud services

1.2 OPC vs Traditional Freelancing

Dimension Freelancer OPC One-Person Company
Revenue Model Hourly/project-based charging Product/subscription/automated revenue
Scalability Highly dependent on personal energy Scale through systems/tools
Legal Entity Individual/sole proprietorship Limited liability company
Asset Accumulation None Yes (product, brand, data)

4. Three Success Paths for OPC Entrepreneurship

4.1 Path 1: Technical Expert with Dimensional Advantage

Suitable for: People with deep technical accumulation who can quickly productize ideas

Key Strategies:

  1. Choose vertical tracks with high technical barriers (e.g., AI + vertical industries)
  2. Use AI to improve development speed by 10x (Claude Code + Cursor combination)
  3. Rapid validation, raise funds for expansion (if needed)

Typical Cases: Xu Zhanwei (smart wristband), Shen Yinghao (3D game “Code Xuan Shu”)

4.2 Path 2: Vertical Niche, Deep Cultivation

Suitable for: People with deep understanding of a certain industry who can identify pain points

Key Strategies:

  1. Find a “niche but刚需 (just-needed)” segment market
  2. Use AI to fill technical gaps (no-code/low-code tools)
  3. Build industry barriers (content + community + data)

Typical Case: Li Jiehui (smart contract review system for law firms)

4.3 Path 3: Service + AI Efficiency, Building Automated Revenue

Suitable for: Content creators, consultants, knowledge workers

Key Strategies:

  1. Standardize and replicat service processes
  2. Use AI to replace repetitive work (customer service, formatting, data analysis)
  3. Convert to subscription/model-based services (e.g., paid Newsletter, online courses)

Typical Cases: Wang Yifeng (public account content automation), Pieter Levels (diversified product matrix)
