3. Monetization Paths: 6 Profit Models for OPC {#monetization-paths}

Path 1: AI Automation Workflow Sales (Monthly Revenue 30K+)

Core Logic: Enterprises have massive repetitive work. You build automation workflows using n8n and sell them as packages.

  • Product Forms: Single template 99-299 RMB, package 199-899 RMB, monthly subscription 299-999 RMB/month, enterprise customization 5,000-50,000 RMB/time
  • Real Case: Individual developer with 4 text processing workflows, 99 RMB/month subscription, 500+ users in 3 months, monthly revenue 30K+
  • Tool List: n8n (free) + DeepSeek (extremely low cost) + Webhook.site (free)

Path 2: Vertical Industry Skill Development (ClawHub App Store)

Opportunity: Generic Skills are saturated, but vertical industry Skills still have huge gaps.

  • Foreign trade businesses need Skills that automatically analyze inquiry emails and generate quotation drafts
  • Restaurant businesses need Skills that automatically adjust menu pricing based on costs
  • Core Principle: Find customers first, then build products

Path 3: AI Content Matrix (Monthly Revenue 2K-10K)

Use AI to boost content production efficiency, exchange platform distribution for traffic, and monetize through traffic conversion. Suitable for people who can continuously produce content, but it’s cyclical. It’s recommended to use it as a customer acquisition leverage rather than a main business.

Path 4: Knowledge-Based AI Q&A Bot (Monthly Subscription Revenue 50K+)

Feed knowledge from a specific domain to AI, let users ask questions anytime, and charge by subscription. Suitable for individuals with professional knowledge and content accumulation.

Path 5: AI Music Copyright (Monthly Passive Income 2K-8K)

Use Suno/Udio to generate basic music tracks, register copyright on platforms like Visual China and Quduoduo, and list on music libraries for video creators to purchase licenses.

Path 6: App Store Small Tools (Explosive Growth Case)

Counterintuitive Case: An indie developer spent months building a complete AI video generator, but no one used it. Instead, a simple app that “splits video in half” gained 60 new users in the past 28 days, with revenue growth of 147%.

💡 Insight: Users don’t want “700 features”, they want “one button to complete the function”. Building small and beautiful tools is easier to succeed than building large and comprehensive platforms.

5. Trend Insights: OPC’s Underlying Logic and Real Challenges {#trend-insights}

5.1 OPC ≠ Traditional Individual Business

Freelancer: Solo fighter, relying on selling personal time and skills for cash. Once work stops, income stops.

OPC (One-Person Company): The smallest practice unit of an AI-native enterprise. The standard architecture is “1 core decision-maker + N AI digital employees + ∞ global collaboration network”. Has independent legal personality and can accumulate commercial assets and credit systems.

5.2 Core Formula

2026 OPC development efficiency formula:

  • I (Idea): Your product creativity and logic loop
  • A (AI Leverage): Your ability to control AI tools (Prompt precision, toolchain integration level)
  • n (Collaboration Coefficient): When using Multi-Agent collaboration, n increases exponentially
  • C (Complexity): Complexity under traditional development models

5.3 Real Challenges: Don’t Misread OPC

⚠️ Common Misconceptions:

  1. Misconception: OPC means “one person does everything”
    Truth: OPC means one person learns to design task flows, then uses AI Agents, automation tools, and external service networks to run tasks

  2. Misconception: To do OPC, you must master AI and control N AI Agents
    Truth: OPC is One Person Company, which is a company. The core of a company is not how efficient the output is, but whether you can capture a demand and satisfy it alone, and someone pays for your service

  3. Misconception: AI is a “magic tool” that can replace the core capabilities of entrepreneurs
    Truth: Agents are “digital assistants” for standardizing repetitive work, not omnipotent “replacements”. Your personal brand + Agent workforce + Automation bus is the complete system

8. Risk Warnings and Compliance Suggestions {#risk-warnings}

⚠️ Investment Requires Caution, Don’t Blindly Follow Trends

  1. Technology Risk: AI tools iterate extremely fast. Today’s popular tool may be eliminated tomorrow. Core capability is “quickly learning new tools”, not “mastering a certain tool”

  2. Market Risk: The OPC track is becoming crowded. Generic tools are already red ocean. You must find vertical scenarios to survive

  3. Compliance Risk: Copyright ownership of AI-generated content, data privacy protection, and industry regulatory requirements need to be understood in advance

  4. Income Volatility: OPC is not “lying down to earn”. You may have zero income in the first 3-6 months. Ensure you have at least 6 months of living expense reserves
