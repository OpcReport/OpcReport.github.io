🧠 III. Theoretical Breakthrough: The Proposal of OPE Concept

3.1 What is OPE?

Dr. Zhang Qi, President of Microsoft AI Asia Pacific, proposed the “OPE (One Person Enterprise)” concept in May 2026—under the deep empowerment of generative AI and agents, individuals are becoming the “minimum innovation system” capable of scheduling tools, systems, and global resources.

OPE Applies to Three Scenarios:

  1. New work methods and organizational forms within large companies
  2. Work method upgrades for teams with fewer than ten people
  3. True personal independent entrepreneurship

The core logic is: with the help of generative AI and agents, individuals can expand their capability boundaries, build virtual teams, complete the full-chain closed loop from creativity to product, and ultimately grow into an efficient and autonomous creation system.

3.2 AI Agents Become Core Productivity

In 2026, AI tools have leapfrogged from auxiliary roles to core productivity units that are programmable, collaborative, and capable of undertaking systematic tasks. By comprehensively utilizing AI super employees (AI Agents), automated tool chains, and cloud services, individual entrepreneurs can build and operate a micro-enterprise covering the entire business chain with unprecedented efficiency.

🔮 V. Trend Outlook: OPC Development Predictions for H2 2026

5.1 Technology Threshold Continues to Decline

The rapid development of domestic large models enables one-person companies to use high-quality AI capabilities at lower costs. The natural advantages in Chinese understanding and localized scenarios will help individual entrepreneurs gain more professional business insights.

Key Capability Upgrades:

  • Content Creation Efficiency: From article writing to visual design, from video editing to voice-over production, work that previously required multi-person collaboration is compressed to one person’s fingertips.
  • Customer Service Capability: Intelligent customer service systems help one-person companies achieve 7x24-hour customer response.
  • Data Analysis and Decision-Making: AI becomes the most reliable “strategist” for one-person companies.

5.2 Creativity and Execution Become Key

As technology thresholds continue to decline, creativity and execution will become the key factors distinguishing excellent one-person companies. AI will not replace entrepreneurs, but it is reshaping the definition of entrepreneurship.

One person, one computer, one set of AI tools—this is becoming the most potential startup configuration in 2026.

5.3 Three-Stage Evolution of the OPC Model

According to the “2026 Global Artificial Intelligence OPC Model Business Insight Report,” the OPC business model will upgrade along the following path:

  1. Project Customization Service Stage: Undertake client needs in a project-based manner, quickly validate business models.
  2. Productized Service Stage: Productize service capabilities to achieve standardized delivery and scalable replication.
  3. Resource Integration Platform Stage: Build a platform ecosystem, integrate upstream and downstream resources, and achieve exponential growth.
