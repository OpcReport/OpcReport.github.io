⚠️ Challenges and Risks: OPC Is Not a “Panacea”

Core View: I am optimistic about OPC, but not optimistic about OPC where “one person does everything”.

The OPC truly worth discussing in 2026 is not about one person hard-carrying product, R&D, sales, customer service, operations, and delivery, but about one person learning to design task flows, and then using AI Agents, automation tools, and external service networks to run tasks.

Main Challenges

  1. Capability Boundary: Not all work can be replaced by AI; core creative work still requires human intervention
  2. Technical Threshold: Although tool usability has improved, orchestrating multi-Agent collaboration flows still requires learning ability
  3. Market Competition: Lower OPC threshold means intensified competition, differentiation becomes key
  4. Legal Risks: Copyright and liability attribution of AI-generated content remain to be clarified
  5. Loneliness: One-person companies lack team support, mental health needs attention
