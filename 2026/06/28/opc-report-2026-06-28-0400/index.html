Executive Summary

The One-Person Company (OPC) model has emerged as a transformative entrepreneurial paradigm in 2026. With local governments actively building OPC ecosystems, successful funding rounds by solo founders, and mature AI tooling, the path from concept to revenue-generating business has never been more accessible for individual entrepreneurs.

1. Market Overview: The Scale of the OPC Revolution

Key Statistics:

  • 1,600万+: Total one-person limited liability companies in China (as of June 2025)
  • 47%: Year-over-year growth in new OPC registrations (H1 2025)
  • 23.8%: Share of new enterprises that are one-person companies
  • 3,000: Dedicated OPC workspaces planned by Chongqing Nan’an District

2. Policy Developments: Government Support for OPC Ecosystems

Chongqing Nan’an: Building a Complete OPC Support System

On June 27, 2026, at the International Innovation & Entrepreneurship Ecology Conference, Chongqing Nan’an District announced ambitious plans:

The Five-Pillar Support System:

  1. Online Platform: Digital service portal enabling “one-click entrepreneurship”
  2. Offline Infrastructure: 20 OPC communities with 3,000 dedicated workstations
  3. Application Scenarios: Four focus tracks—digital content, gaming/esports, industry software, and AI agent development
  4. Professional Services: One-stop solutions for business registration, tax, and intellectual property
  5. Physical Space: Youth talent apartments creating a “campus-park-apartment” closed loop

The “One Desk, One Bed, One Investment” Model

Chongqing’s “Starry Sky” action plan implements a revolutionary support framework:

  • One Desk: 50 free OPC workstations, 800m² presentation hall, meeting rooms at zero cost
  • One Bed: Youth apartments within walking distance, housing subsidies available
  • One Investment: “Invest first, equity later” model—no repayment or equity dilution during early stages

7. Challenges and Risk Management

7.1 Service Infrastructure Gaps

Current pain points identified by practitioners:

  • Business registration, office leasing, software copyright filing consume significant time
  • Information asymmetry leads to overcharging by intermediaries
  • Traditional service providers prioritize sales over genuine support

Emerging Opportunity: OPC-focused service providers offering transparent, fairly-priced compliance solutions.

7.2 AI Tool Dependency Risks

  • Model hallucinations and output instability
  • API service interruptions
  • Homogenization and competitive copying

Mitigation Strategy: Build moats on unique data, sophisticated AI workflows, deep domain knowledge, and personal brand—not just tool usage.

7.3 Isolation and Sustainability

One-person operations risk burnout and cognitive limitations. Solutions include:

  • Participating in OPC communities
  • Building in Public (sharing progress openly)
  • Collaborating with other “super individuals” on projects

8. The 90-Day Launch Roadmap

Phase 1: Days 1-30 - Positioning & MVP

Focus Areas:

  • Track selection: AI-efficient domains (AIGC content, smart SEO, niche SaaS, knowledge products)
  • MVP construction: 2-4 week development using no-code/low-code platforms
  • Tool stack setup: Core AI tools configuration

Recommended Tech Stack:

  • Content: GPT-4 / Claude
  • Development: Cursor / Trae
  • Automation: Make / n8n
  • Analytics: Mixpanel / PostHog

Phase 2: Days 31-60 - Launch & Customer Acquisition

Key Activities:

  • Content-driven acquisition: AI-powered high-volume content production
  • Smart sales: Website chatbots for lead capture
  • Data-driven operations: Monitor key metrics with AI-assisted analysis

Phase 3: Days 61-90 - Optimization & Systematization

Focus Areas:

  • Process automation: Eliminate repetitive work
  • AI Agent team building: Configure specialized agents for core functions
  • Model replication: Expand to related niche markets after validation

9. Key Insights for Solo Founders

The Paradigm Shift

We’re witnessing a fundamental transformation from “organizations hiring individuals” to “individuals empowering organizations.”

Three Critical Trends

  1. Policy Acceleration: Local governments actively building OPC ecosystems with subsidized space and capital support
  2. Technology Maturity: AI Agents moving from concept to practice as virtual team members
  3. Success Validation: Proven cases like Seede AI demonstrating the viability of solo-founder funding rounds

Success Formula

The core of success is not having all skills, but the ability to effectively integrate and orchestrate AI resources.

10. Looking Ahead

The “core-satellite network” model is emerging: a super individual (core) orchestrating global resources and AI employees (satellites) through intelligent tools and contracts, achieving high-efficiency, low-cost business operations.

This represents the most significant shift in entrepreneurial paradigms since the industrial revolution—the democratization of entrepreneurship through AI empowerment.
