1. What is OPC? Why Now? {#what-is-opc}

1.1 Core Definition of OPC

OPC (One-Person Company) refers to individual entrepreneurs who leverage AI capabilities to independently complete the full-cycle business loop—from product conception and design to R&D and marketing—starting a business in a “one-person-supports-a-whole-team” mode.

🧬 The Core Logic of OPC:

Carbon-Based Wisdom (Human) + Silicon-Based Execution (AI) = Complete Company

  • Human does decision/creativity/strategy: Define boundaries, orchestrate logic, judge value
  • AI does execution/standardization/processes: Code generation, content creation, data analysis, customer service

1.2 Why OPC Exploded in 2026

💡 The Turning Point: The collective upgrade of Large Language Model (LLM) capabilities in 2025 enabled people with zero coding knowledge to independently complete entire project R&D. This is the real reason OPC exploded in 2026.

Three Core Drivers:

  1. AI Tool Maturity (2024-2026): Evolution from “code completion” to “Intent-Driven” systematic development
  2. Threshold Completely Shattered: Past SaaS products requiring 5-person teams (frontend, backend, UI, testing, product) can now be completed by one person with AI toolchains in 48 hours from project initiation to launch
  3. Cost Reduced to 1%: Numerous solo entrepreneurship projects have emerged in Silicon Valley, with R&D cycles compressed from months to days, and costs reduced to 1% of traditional teams

On June 20, 2026, four departments of Hainan Province jointly issued the Artificial Intelligence OPC One-Person Company Special Support Policy, creating an AI-enabled “super individual” entrepreneurship ecosystem.

🎁 Core Policy Dividends:

  • Tax Incentives: Corporate income tax 15%, personal income tax 15% (dual 15% incentive)
  • Limited Liability Protection: 100% holding by one person, no need for partners, zero decision-making friction
  • Free Office Space: OPC communities provide rent-free, move-in-ready workstations
  • Computing Power Subsidies: Quality communities can receive up to 8 million yuan in provincial-level rewards
  • Fully Online Registration: Fastest 1-day certificate issuance, no physical venue required

5. The Evolution of OPC Communities: From Office Space to Ecosystem Collaboration {#opc-communities}

In 2026, OPC communities are gradually evolving from simple office spaces or entrepreneurship groups to new organizational carriers connecting AI tools, individual entrepreneurs, industrial resources, and enterprise application scenarios.

  • Resource Sharing: Computing power subsidies, office space, legal services, financing connections
  • Complementary Capabilities: OPC entrepreneurs in different fields can outsource tasks to each other
  • Brand Endorsement: Joining a quality OPC community equals gaining credit enhancement

6. Challenges and Risks: OPC Is Not a Panacea {#challenges}

🚫 What I DON’T Favor: OPC where “one person does everything”

I favor OPC (One-Person Company), but I do NOT favor OPC where “one person stubbornly carries product, R&D, sales, customer service, operations, and delivery.” The truly valuable OPC discussion in 2026 centers on:

  1. One person learning to design task flows
  2. Then using AI Agents, automation tools, and external service networks to run tasks
  3. Letting AI undertake standardized, repetitive work

6.1 Scenarios Suitable for OPC

  • ✅ SaaS product development and operations
  • ✅ Content creation and knowledge monetization
  • ✅ Vertical domain consulting services
  • ✅ Cross-border e-commerce independent sites
  • ✅ AI tool integration and customization

6.2 Scenarios NOT Suitable for OPC

  • ❌ Services requiring large amounts of offline manual delivery
  • ❌ Manufacturing requiring heavy asset investment
  • ❌ Ventures requiring complex supply chain management
  • ❌ Core businesses in strongly regulated industries (e.g., finance, healthcare)

6.3 How Can Ordinary People Catch the OPC Dividend?

  1. Find a niche track: Don’t aim to build “the next OpenAI” right away—find a segmented problem you can solve
  2. Learn to orchestrate AI: In 2026, AI tools are not lacking; what’s lacking are people who know how to use them
  3. Build personal brand: Your core asset in OPC is your personal brand and trustworthiness
  4. Leverage policy dividends: Hainan, Yangtze River Delta, Pearl River Delta all have OPC special support policies
  5. Join OPC communities: Don’t entrepreneurship alone—find your “digital colleague” network

7. Future Outlook: 2026 Second Half OPC Trend Predictions {#future-outlook}

🔮 Key Trends for H2 2026:

  1. Multi-Agent collaboration becomes mainstream: Evolution from single AI tool usage to multi-agent collaborative workflows
  2. OPC specialized division of labor: “OPC service providers” will emerge (e.g., OPC legal, OPC finance, OPC marketing)
  3. Passive income普及 (Popularization of passive income): Achieving “sleep income” models through AI automation
  4. OPC integration with traditional enterprises: Traditional enterprises begin purchasing OPC AI solutions
  5. Continued policy support escalation: More cities are expected to introduce OPC special support policies

8. Conclusion: OPC Is Evolution, Not Regression {#conclusion}

OPC (One-Person Company) is NOT “regressing to individual entrepreneurship,” but rather a major evolution of business organization forms in the AI era. It reconstructs the organizational model of traditional entrepreneurship, with the core essence being “single-person led, AI implemented.”

AI undertakes all standardized, repetitive work, while entrepreneurs focus on core decision-making, innovation breakthroughs, and value judgment—this is the essence of OPC, and the new paradigm that every entrepreneur in 2026 needs to deeply understand.

🎯 Action Recommendations

If you are seriously considering OPC entrepreneurship, now is the best time. But please remember:

  • OPC is NOT “one person stubbornly carrying everything,” but “one person + AI team”
  • In 2026, what matters is NOT how much AI technology you know, but whether you know how to leverage AI’s power
  • Find a small niche, hand over miscellaneous tasks like writing, formatting, and publishing to AI, and interface with clients yourself
  • Time investment is worth it: Learning to use Cursor, LangGraph, v0.dev will exponentially increase your productivity
