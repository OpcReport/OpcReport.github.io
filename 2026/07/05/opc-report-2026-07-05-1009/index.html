🔥 Weekly Highlights

💥 Global OPC Co-Creation Festival: A New Paradigm for the AI Era

The Global OPC Co-Creation Festival, an original exhibition segment, gathered One-Person Company representatives from content marketing, music creation and other tracks, presenting a new landscape of entrepreneurship under the AI wave. With technical barriers broken down, one person can now handle entire business chains that previously required team collaboration — dramatically lowering the barrier to innovation.

🚀 Hainan Free Trade Port: 8 Million RMB Special Subsidy for AI OPC

Hainan province’s four departments jointly issued a special support policy for AI OPC (One-Person Company), supercharging the “Super Individual” entrepreneurship ecosystem with FTZ tax benefits, cross-border advantages, and venue subsidies. The policy took effect June 20, 2026, running through December 31, 2028.

🌍 Sweden’s Lovable: $100M ARR in 8 Months, $6.6B Valuation

Sweden’s Lovable achieved $100 million annualized revenue in just 8 months, with valuations surging to $6.6 billion — the brightest unicorn story in the global OPC track this year.

🎯 Top 7 Hot Startup Sectors

1. AI Digital Employee Platforms

Beijing Future Tide Technology launched an AI digital employee engineering delivery platform, helping SMEs build proprietary digital employees and creating digital avatars for experts. Founder Liu Yefei, with 18 years of tech architecture experience, now runs the company with 5 “AI employees” handling strategy coordination, code writing, code review, automated cloud deployment, and real-time information gathering.

2. Short Drama Industrialization

AI tools have completely disrupted traditional production workflows. One independent creator produced an 88-minute AI-generated micro-drama in just 3 months, with one person operating the entire process and costs controlled at the 10,000 RMB level.

3. Virtual Idol Economy

Core functions like character modeling, voice synthesis, and music production can all be accomplished through AI tools. A UK programmer’s virtual streamer, powered by AI-generated singing and interactive content, gained over 1 million followers on overseas platforms.

4. AI Music Creation

A Qingdao entrepreneur reduced music demo production time from one week to five minutes using AI tools, with projected annual revenue exceeding 1.5 million RMB. Companies are also compressing voice cloning costs to one-thousandth of traditional methods.

5. Vertical SaaS Tools

Solo founders + AI Agents are building vertical e-commerce, enterprise collaboration tools, local life SaaS, and content distribution platforms. R&D cycles compressed from months to days, costs reduced to 1% of traditional teams.

6. AI Prompt Engineering

The core of “using AI” is “commanding AI” — AI prompt engineers are emerging as a new role, optimizing instructions for AI tools to generate precisely what users need. Think of it as an “AI translator.”

7. Cross-Border E-commerce

With Shopify and AI customer service, AI translation, and AI marketing tools, independent developers can build cross-border e-commerce businesses from scratch with zero prior experience.

🏆 OPC Independent Pioneer Challenge

The World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC) and OpenCSG launched the OPC Independent Pioneer Challenge, the world’s first top-tier incubation platform specifically designed for super-individuals and AI entrepreneurs. This is not just a competition — it’s a complete ecosystem for discovering potential, linking resources, and cultivating future unicorns.

🔮 2026 Trend Outlook

Human + AI Agents (2025–2026)

AI agents help individuals and small businesses automate business processes, improving efficiency and productivity. Solo founders use AI to replace traditional co-founders — without emotional fluctuations, never quitting, avoiding conflicts.

Human + Agent Swarms (2026–2027)

Not only single tasks, but entire workflows and multiple interconnected workflows can be completed through collaboration of AI agent swarms, providing unprecedented flexibility for entrepreneurs to rapidly adjust products and services based on market demands.

Human + Autonomous Companies (2027+)

Autonomous companies are not just organizations with AI agent support; they are completely self-managing business entities that can independently make strategic decisions, optimize operations, and respond to market changes.
