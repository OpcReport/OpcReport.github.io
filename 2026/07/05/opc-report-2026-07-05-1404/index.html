In 2026, the “Smart Economy New Form” was written into the Government Work Report for the first time, marking the third consecutive year of the “AI+” strategy deployment. On June 18, seven ministries including MIIT jointly released the Platform Economy Collaborative Development Action Plan (2026–2028), explicitly calling for “accelerating the cultivation of AI-powered one-person companies.” This marks the first time OPC has been formally endorsed at the central government level.

  • 10+ OPC communities, each ≥10,000 sqm
  • 1,000 high-growth AI startups
  • Startup funds up to ¥1 million, talent relocation subsidies up to ¥100K, R&D subsidies up to ¥10 million in “compute vouchers”
  • Low-cost workspace and subsidized housing

🌊 Shanghai: Lingang “Super Individual 288” Base

As the birthplace of China’s OPC movement, Shanghai’s Lingang area launched the “Super Individual 288” base offering zero-rent workspace, compute subsidies, and free AI tools. Zhangjiang AI Town also established an OPC incubation center.

🤖 3. AI Tool Ecosystem: The Year of the Digital Employee

🦞 OpenClaw (Lobster): AI Agent Takes the World by Storm

2026 is called the “Year One of the Digital Employee.” OpenClaw (nicknamed 龙虾/Lobster 🦞) has shattered traditional coding barriers. Key milestones:

  • June 29: OpenClaw launched on iOS and Android simultaneously (alongside Cursor’s mobile beta)
  • This marks AI Agent’s transition from “sitting at a desk” to ubiquitous, always-on mobile operation
  • Current stable: v2.7+
  • Cross-platform: Windows / macOS / Linux
  • Multi-model: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, Ollama, more
  • “Local-first, privacy-first” — all data stays on your machine

The ecosystem has generated hundreds of tutorials, deployment guides, and community resources (learnclaw.cc, etc.) in Chinese tech communities.

📘 Anthropic’s Founder’s Playbook

Released in May 2026, this 36-page PDF systematically addresses how startups should redefine organization, product, R&D, and growth in the age of AI Agents with “employee-like capabilities.” Core thesis: AI has eliminated technical barriers — a great idea can go further than ever before.

🤝 Claude Tag: AI Joins the Team

In June 2026, Anthropic introduced Claude Tag for Slack. Claude is no longer a separate chat window — you can now @Claude within team workflows. This marks AI’s evolution from “a tool people use” to “a team member people collaborate with.”

🐍 Python + AI Agent: Full-Stack Automation Becomes OPC Standard

More OPC entrepreneurs are building full-stack automation workflows with Python + AI Agents. From content production and customer service to data analysis and auto-deployment — one person + one AI system now covers the entire business chain.

🧠 5. Trend Outlook & Founder Recommendations

🔮 Five Key Trends for H2 2026

  1. Policy dividend wave continues — After Hainan/Shenzhen/Shanghai precedent, more cities will introduce OPC-specific policies
  2. AI Agent goes mobile — With OpenClaw/Cursor on phones, “raising AI employees anywhere” becomes normal
  3. OPC community ecosystem matures — From workspace to incubation to investment, the full OPC pipeline is forming
  4. “Skills + IP” becomes the moat — AI flattens technical access; deep expertise and trust become the true differentiators
  5. AI Agent collaboration becomes team-level — Products like Claude Tag elevate AI from point tool to collaborative team member

⚠️ Risk Advisory for OPC Founders

  • AI inference costs, while dropping fast, remain a long-term operational consideration
  • Solo entrepreneurship brings psychological challenges — join OPC communities for support
  • Lower technical barriers = more competition — differentiation and personal IP are more critical than ever
  • Some government policies are still rolling out — verify local OPC community conditions before relocating
  • Start with an MVP, validate demand, then scale. Don’t FOMO into big spending.
