2. What is OPC? {#what-is-opc}

AI-powered OPC (One-Person Company) refers to a new entrepreneurial model where individual entrepreneurs can independently complete the full-chain closed loop of product design, R&D, operations, and customer service, relying on AI technology.

Core Characteristics

  • AI Handles Repetitive Tasks: Entrepreneurs focus on strategic decision-making and creative innovation
  • Digital Nativity: Built from 0 to 1 entirely on digital infrastructure
  • Capability Composition: One person with multiple skills, AI填补s capability gaps
  • Efficiency Leap: 8-10x efficiency improvement compared to traditional teams
  • Ecosystem Dependence: Relies on external ecosystems like large models, computing power, and communities
  • Scenario-Driven: Starts from specific pain points, rapid iteration

Three Major Classifications

  1. Model Innovation Type: Reconstructs traditional industry processes, such as AI digital employee platforms
  2. Scenario Application Type: Focuses on vertical scenarios, such as short drama creation, virtual idols
  3. Ecosystem Service Type: Provides tools, training, and resource matching for OPCs

8. Challenges and Solutions {#challenges}

Major Challenges

  • Insufficient Personal Energy: Technical R&D, market operations, customer communication, solution design, roadshow participation, and other tasks are all undertaken by one person
  • Lack of AI Self-Checking Closed Loop: When AI makes mistakes, humans can correct them correspondingly; AI currently cannot achieve self-checking closed loop
  • Cognitive Traps: AI-only theory, capability misestimation, legal risks, fake efforts

Success Factors

  • Vertical Track Differentiation Capability: True competitiveness lies in deep cultivation of vertical tracks
  • Compliance and Risk Control: Legal risks cannot be ignored
  • Team Collaboration Awareness: One-person company is just a stage, not the endpoint. Only by gathering like-minded partners can one embark on a path of scalable entrepreneurship

10. Conclusion {#conclusion}

In 2026, OPC one-person companies are transitioning from concept to reality, moving from “tool assistance” to “autonomous entities,” from “industry empowerment” to “ecosystem reconstruction.”

When AI no longer just answers questions and executes instructions, but can independently produce, collaborate, socialize, and make decisions, one-person companies will become one of the most mainstream entrepreneurial models.

For entrepreneurs, the key is to identify vertical tracks, build differentiation capabilities, and implement compliance risk control, while maintaining an open mindset and introducing teams at appropriate times to achieve the leap from “one-person company” to “scalable enterprise.”

AI is the greatest dividend for OPC entrepreneurs. Technological progress drives productivity and organizational form reform. OPCs with efficient and agile characteristics will play a key role in this process!
