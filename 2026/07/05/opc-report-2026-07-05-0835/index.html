🚀 OPC One-Person Company AI Entrepreneurship Trend Report - July 2026

Super Individual Rise in Progress: From Concept to Reality in Entrepreneurship

  • Solo-founded Company Ratio: From 23.7% (2019) to 36.3% (2025 H1) — over 50% increase
  • Domestic OPC Entrepreneur Scale: Surpassed 10 million
  • Market Size Prediction: OPC-related market expected to exceed 800 billion yuan in 2026
  • Policy Support: 20+ cities launched OPC-specific support policies
  • Hainan Free Trade Port OPC Policy: China’s first provincial-level OPC support package (free workspace + computing power subsidy + dual 15% tax incentives)

🔥 July Hot Events Tracker

1. Global OPC Co-Creation Festival Successfully Held

On July 4, 2026, as an innovative section of a major expo, the Global OPC Co-Creation Festival brought together “One-Person Company” representatives from content marketing, music creation, and other tracks, showcasing the new entrepreneurship landscape under the AI wave.

Key Insight: AI tools are reshaping production methods. After technological barriers were broken, one person can now complete full-chain businesses that previously required team collaboration, dramatically lowering innovation thresholds.

2. WAIC OPC Independent Pioneer Challenge

The World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC), in collaboration with OpenCSG, launched the OPC Independent Pioneer Challenge, becoming the world’s first top-tier incubation platform dedicated to super individuals and AI entrepreneurs.

💡 Core Logic: "Carbon-based Wisdom (Human) + Silicon-based Execution (AI) = Complete Company"

3. Beijing Future Tide Technology: Entrepreneurship with 5 “AI Employees”

Liu Yefei, founder of Beijing Future Tide Technology Co., Ltd., with 18 years of experience in technical architecture and management at internet companies, is now entrepreneurship with 5 “AI employees”:

  • Solution coordination and decision support
  • Code writing
  • Code review
  • Cloud server automated deployment
  • Real-time information collection, market dynamics, and investment advice

In 2026, the company launched an AI Digital Employee Engineering Delivery Platform, helping SMEs build exclusive digital employees and enabling experts to create digital avatars.

Development Tools

  • GitHub Copilot / Cursor / Claude Code: Complete 80% of code writing and debugging
  • v0.dev: From idea to UI prototype, generate in seconds
  • Retool / FlutterFlow: Low-code platforms for rapid MVP application building
  • Python + AI Agent: Full-stack automation in practice (complete code open-source projects now available)

Content Creation Tools

  • General text LLMs + vertical short-drama tools: automated scriptwriting, character design, storyboard generation
  • AI digital human generation + AI music production: one-stop exclusive content creation
  • Virtual streamer creation tools: complete character design in thousands of “gacha” attempts

📈 Five High-Potential Tracks (July 2026 Update)

1. AI Agent Applications

Composite Score: 9.9/10

Keywords: Automation, high compound interest, low marginal cost

Case Study: E-commerce operators deploy Agents to automatically analyze competitor main images, generate copy, test CTR — fully automated without human intervention.

Individual Opportunities:

  • Package Agents for vertical scenarios (social media scheduling, customer service, data cleaning)
  • Provide “out-of-the-box” lightweight SaaS with monthly subscription fees
  • Use LangChain, LlamaIndex, and other frameworks to rapidly build prototypes

2. AI Going Global

Core Advantage: Overseas users more willing to pay for efficiency tools, with relatively weaker competition

Existing Cases: Multiple Chinese teams’ AI writing, image generation, and video editing tools achieving stable monthly revenue on App Store and Stripe.

Action Recommendations:

  • Select products with existing Chinese MVPs, translate + localize before launching overseas
  • Focus on niche scenarios: independent site SEO optimization, TikTok content generation, Shopify plugins
  • Utilize platforms like Gumroad, Paddle for payment processing

3. Short Drama Creation (AI-Driven)

Disruption Point: Short drama production that previously required writers, directors, cinematographers, post-production, and other multi-person collaboration can now be completed by one person.

Case Study: An independent creator spent three months completing an 88-minute AI live-action short drama, operated entirely by one person, with costs controlled at the tens of thousands yuan level.

4. Virtual Idol/Virtual Streamer

Model: “Solo Talent Agency” — character modeling, voice synthesis, and music production all achieved through AI tools.

Case Study: A virtual streamer developed by a British programmer, with AI-generated singing and interactive content, gained over one million followers on overseas platforms.

5. Game Development (Lightweight)

Transformation: Commercial games that traditionally required dozens of team members and millions in budget can now be completed by one person.

Key Tools: AI-assisted programming + game engine templates + asset generation AI

⚠️ Three Major Traps in OPC Entrepreneurship

Trap 1: Tool Hoarding Syndrome

Symptoms: Bought copywriting tools, drawing software, subscribed to various APIs, yet still trapped in cross-system logins, data transfer, and trivial process integration.

Cure: Focus on core task flows, select 2-3 tools that can connect into a complete workflow, rather than collecting all trending tools.

Trap 2: One Person Shouldering All Responsibilities

Symptoms: Product, R&D, sales, customer service, operations, delivery — all done by yourself, burning out.

Cure: The truly viable OPC model in 2026 isn’t “one person doing everything,” but rather “one person learning to design task flows, then using AI Agents, automation tools, and external service networks to execute tasks.”

Trap 3: Ignoring Compliance and Taxation

Risk: Although OPC is small, legal issues like taxation, data compliance, and intellectual property protection cannot be ignored.

Recommendation: Use AI legal tools (such as contract review AI) for basic compliance checks, and consult professional lawyers when necessary.

🔮 Second Half 2026 Trend Predictions

Prediction 1: OPC Moves from “Concept Validation” to “Large-Scale Implementation”

The first half of the year was a period of doubt about “can one person + AI accomplish things,” but the second half will see numerous OPC entrepreneurs’ revenue break through the million-yuan, or even ten-million-yuan level.

Prediction 2: “AI Employee” Management Platforms Become New Trend

How to manage multiple AI Agents, how to evaluate AI work efficiency, how to enable AI employees to collaborate — these demands will spur new SaaS categories.

Prediction 3: Traditional SaaS Vendors Will Be Disrupted by OPC

AI-native cloud platforms like OpenClaw enable solo developers to complete full-stack development, product operations, and customer service. “One-person companies disrupting vertical industries” is becoming reality from slogan, and traditional SaaS vendor stock prices have already collectively plummeted.

Prediction 4: AI Autonomous Social Networks Rise

The MoltBook case (pure AI Agent social network) shows that AI autonomous posting, interaction, and forming communities and public opinion ecosystems have become possible. 100K+ Agents flooded in within 48 hours, with tens of millions of users watching.

💼 Three Recommendations for OPC Entrepreneurs

  1. Find Your “Leverage Point”: Not all processes need AI-ification. Find the part you’re best at and can create the most value, and use AI to amplify it.

  2. Build “Task Flow” Thinking: Don’t think “I need to learn all AI tools,” but rather “in my work process, which steps can be completed by AI.”

  3. Join Communities, Don’t Fight Alone: OPC isn’t isolated combat. OPC communities, developer forums, and industry networking events are all great places to get feedback and find collaboration opportunities.
