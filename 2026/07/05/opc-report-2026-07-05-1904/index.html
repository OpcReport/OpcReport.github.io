7. Practical Advice for OPC Entrepreneurs

✅ Three Questions to Clarify Before Starting

  1. What is your core competitiveness? OPC is not about doing everything yourself, but focusing on your strongest area
  2. Which parts can be replaced by AI? List all workflows and identify parts that can be automated
  3. How to build credibility? The biggest challenge for one-person companies is trust, which needs to be established through content, case studies, and word-of-mouth

⚠️ Common Pitfalls of OPC

  • Trying to do everything yourself: AI is an assistant, not omnipotent; some things still need outsourcing or collaboration
  • Ignoring legal and tax issues: One-person companies also need compliant operations, cannot be ignored because it’s “one person”
  • Lack of systematization: Many OPC entrepreneurs stay at the “manual AI tuning” stage, with a low efficiency ceiling
  • Loneliness: Solo entrepreneurship can be lonely; need to actively join communities and build networks
