🏛️ Policy & Ecosystem Updates

🟢 Hainan Free Trade Port OPC Policy Officially Launched

Hainan Province’s four-department joint OPC (One Person Company) Special Support Policy officially took effect on June 20, 2026, valid through December 31, 2028. This policy layers Hainan FTP’s traditional tax benefits, cross-border advantages, and venue subsidies to create an unprecedented policy environment for AI-powered light-asset entrepreneurship.

Key Policy Highlights:

  • Full opening of cross-border e-commerce one-person companies
  • Stacked FTP tax incentives
  • Venue rental subsidies
  • Hainan becomes the 5th major city after Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, and Suzhou to fully bet on the “Super Individual”

🟢 Alibaba Cloud “OPC Innovation Support Plan” Launches

Alibaba Cloud unveiled a comprehensive support plan for 5,000 AI innovation teams nationwide, offering five major support dimensions:

  1. Token Subsidy: Up to ¥1,000,000 RMB in Token support (tiered distribution)
  2. Dedicated Tech Service: One-on-one technical support + full-lifecycle product service
  3. Alibaba Ecosystem Access: Traffic entry points via Taobao, Alipay, Alibaba Cloud Market
  4. Brand Support: Visibility opportunities at Alibaba’s Yunqi Conference and other top events
  5. Financing Access: Direct access to top VC closed-door roadshows

🟢 WAIC 2026 Confirmed: July 17–20, Shanghai

The World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC) 2026 has been officially confirmed for July 17–20 in Shanghai, with the theme “Intelligent Partners, Co-Creating the Future.” The conference will link three major districts, feature seven thematic areas, and aggregate global industry-academia-research capital resources. A Singapore Asia-Pacific event will follow in August.

💡 AI Agent Cost Revolution: From “Sky High” to “Dirt Cheap”

From 2024 to 2026, the operating cost of equivalent-capability AI Agents dropped 128x — from $5,000/month to under $40/month.

What this means for OPC founders:

  • One AI Agent’s daily task throughput equals 3–5 full-time employees
  • AI execution costs are only 1/10 or less of traditional human labor

👁️ Industry Deep Dive

🎯 From “Tool User” to “AI Company Founder”: A Paradigm Shift

2026 marks a pivotal shift in AI entrepreneurship focus. Early-stage discussions centered on “can I use AI tools?” — now the market asks “can I convert AI tools into deliverable products, content, or services?”

This signals AI entrepreneurship has moved from the experimental phase into large-scale commercial deployment.

🏗️ The OPC Pyramid Structure Is Forming

Current OPC enterprises show a classic pyramid structure:

  • Bottom Layer (76.3%): Basic services — content creation, social media ops, translation, customer service
  • Middle Layer (18.7%): Tech development — SaaS products, AI applications, vertical tools
  • Top Layer (5%): High complexity — intelligent agent platforms, platform-level applications
