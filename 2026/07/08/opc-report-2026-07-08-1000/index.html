⚠️ Three Major Pitfalls of OPC Entrepreneurship

🚨 One Hit and You’re Done!

  • ❌ Technology Illusion: Believing you can build any product with AI, ignoring the establishment of technology barriers
  • ❌ Customer Illusion: Believing good products attract customers, ignoring the core challenge of market acquisition
  • ❌ Time Illusion: Believing AI handles everything, ignoring the finiteness of personal energy and time

Core Reminder: AI changes efficiency, not the underlying business logic. Market behaviors like customer acquisition still require human involvement.
