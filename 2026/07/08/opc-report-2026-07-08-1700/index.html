1. 🌍 Global OPC Co-Creation Festival Held at 2026 Global Digital Economy Conference

Date: July 6, 2026

The 2026 Global Digital Economy Conference in Shanghai featured the world’s first Global OPC (One-Person Company) Co-Creation Festival — a dedicated celebration for solopreneurs leveraging AI. Representatives from content marketing, music creation, and other sectors showcased how AI tools are reshaping the entrepreneurial landscape. The festival highlighted that with technical barriers broken, one person can now deliver an entire business chain that previously required a full team.

“In the wave of AI, you’re not fighting alone.” — Global OPC Co-Creation Festival

2. 🏛️ Seven Chinese Ministries Jointly Issue Policy to Accelerate AIOPC Development

Date: June 2026

In a landmark policy move, China’s Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT) and six other ministries jointly issued a directive explicitly calling to accelerate the cultivation of AIOPC (AI One-Person Companies). Over 20 Chinese cities have introduced special OPC support policies, with Shanghai, Jiangsu, Guangdong, and others incorporating OPC into their “15th Five-Year Plan” strategic blueprints — marking AI solopreneurship’s official transition from individual exploration to national strategic layout.

4. 📘 Anthropic Releases “Founder’s Playbook”: AI Eliminates Technical Barriers

Date: May 14, 2026

Anthropic published a 36-page PDF handbook — “The Founder’s Playbook: Building an AI-Native Startup” — making a hard prediction for 2026: “AI eliminates technical barriers; a great idea goes further than ever before.” The core thesis: the fundamental shift in 2026 isn’t about business model innovation, it’s about a complete transformation of the productivity paradigm.

“In the AI era, one person can build a $1 billion unicorn company.” — Sam Altman, OpenAI CEO

5. 🏆 “Year One of the One-Person Company”: 2026 Industry Designation

Generative AI, multimodal tools, and AI Agent technology have matured to push individual productivity to historic highs. One person equipped with an AI toolchain can now deliver products and services that previously required 10+ person teams. According to China’s State Administration for Market Regulation, as of mid-2025, China’s one-person company stock exceeded 16 million, accounting for 27.4% of all enterprises nationwide.

⚠️ AIOPC Gold Rush: Dividends, Traps & Survival Rules

✅ Three Major Dividends

  1. Technical barriers eliminated: AI tools enable individuals to complete full-chain business operations previously requiring teams
  2. Policy dividends: MIIT + 7-ministry joint directive, 20+ city support policies
  3. Toolchain maturity: Claude Code, Dify, etc. give individuals enterprise-grade delivery capability

⛔ Three Major Traps

  1. General-purpose assistant track is monopolized by tech giants: compute clusters + data flywheel + ecosystem moats — individual entry = “training wheels”
  2. Long monetization cycles: Vertical SaaS / AI platforms need 6–12 months before first revenue
  3. Pure content mass production is no longer enough: Need deep industry expertise as moat

✅ Survival Rules

  1. Choose “short monetization path” tracks — first paying customer within 0–3 months
  2. OPC moat = professional depth, not “doing everything”
  3. Focus on vertical scenarios + strong workflow embedding + industry Know-How
  4. “Stay alive first, future comes later”
