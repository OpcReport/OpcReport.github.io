⚠️ AIOPC Gold Rush: Dividends, Traps & Survival Rules

✅ Three Major Dividends

  1. Technical barriers eliminated: AI tools enable individuals to complete full-chain business operations previously requiring teams
  2. Policy dividends: MIIT + 7-ministry joint directive, 20+ city support policies
  3. Toolchain maturity: Claude Code, Dify, etc. give individuals enterprise-grade delivery capability

⛔ Three Major Traps

  1. General-purpose assistant track is monopolized by tech giants: compute clusters + data flywheel + ecosystem moats — individual entry = “training wheels”
  2. Long monetization cycles: Vertical SaaS / AI platforms need 6–12 months before first revenue
  3. Pure content mass production is no longer enough: Need deep industry expertise as moat

✅ Survival Rules

  1. Choose “short monetization path” tracks — first paying customer within 0–3 months
  2. OPC moat = professional depth, not “doing everything”
  3. Focus on vertical scenarios + strong workflow embedding + industry Know-How
  4. “Stay alive first, future comes later”
