⚠️ Survival Rules: Challenges & Traps Facing OPCs

🚨 Trap #1: AI Tool Hoarding

Many entrepreneurs fall into the “AI tool proliferation” trap: buying copywriting tools, design software, subscribing to various APIs—yet still getting stuck in cross-system login, data transfer, and tedious process integration.

Solution: Build a unified AI Agent task-flow system. Stop accumulating tools; start orchestrating workflows.

🔑 Four Survival Rules for OPCs in 2026

  1. 🎯 Task-Flow Design First
    Don’t try to handle product/development/sales/customer service/operations/delivery alone. Learn to design task flows and let AI Agents run them.

  2. 🧠 Expand Your “Manageable Radius”
    AI is the first technology to truly expand what a single person can manage. Shift from executor to decision-maker.

  3. 🌐 Leverage External Service Networks
    Determine: Which tasks get AI assistance? Which require human judgment? Which should be outsourced to professional services?

  4. 📈 Full-Chain Closed-Loop Thinking
    Product R&D + customer acquisition + content + sales + service + delivery + finance + review + growth. Break any link, and the business stalls.
