China’s first collective standard for AI OPC terminology officially released. For the first time, a clear national definition has been established:

AI OPC (Artificial Intelligence One-Person Company): A company led by 1 core natural person with generally no more than 10 employees, primarily engaged in AI technology research, development, application, or services.
