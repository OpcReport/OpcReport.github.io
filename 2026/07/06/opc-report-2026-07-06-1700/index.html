⚠️ Risk Warnings: The Six “Deaths” of OPC

⚡ Early Adopters Beginning to “Exit” in Batches

The earliest “AI cyber饼” (AI hype) adopters in the One-Person Company space have already started exiting in batches. Research shows that while OPC has advantages like low cost, high efficiency, and fast decision-making, it still requires clearing obstacles in business models, customer acquisition, costs, and profitability in actual operations.

🚨 Six High-Risk Traps

# Trap Description
1 Over-reliance on AI Chat Only using chat interface instead of Agent Swarm to manage digital teams
2 Unclear Business Model No sustainable monetization path identified
