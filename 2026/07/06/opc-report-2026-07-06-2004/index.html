🎯 Headline: Global OPC Co-Creation Festival Opens - One-Person Companies Get Their Own Stage

Today, at the 2026 Global Digital Economy Conference, a “Global OPC (One Person Company) Co-Creation Festival” specially designed for one-person company entrepreneurs officially launched. This marks the first time the OPC concept has received dedicated agenda space at a national-level digital economy forum, signaling that the one-person company model has formally entered the mainstream.

Core Signal: If you’re an AI entrepreneur, this festival will make you feel that amidst the surging AI wave, you’re not fighting alone. One-person companies are no longer synonymous with “small-scale operations” - they are a recognized, supported, and empowered new business paradigm.

🤖 Deep Dive: How AI Agents Are Reshaping One-Person Company Business Models

With the leap in Agent technology, AI can now not only execute instructions but also actively plan tasks, call tools, and even perform cross-system collaboration. This means:

  • Rise of Digital Employees: One person plus a few agents can build a business operational loop that previously required an entire company
  • Role Redefinition: Agents handle customer inquiries, generate contract drafts, monitor market dynamics, while humans only need to sit in the decision-maker’s seat for key judgments
  • Scale Possibility: One-person companies for the first time possess the technical infrastructure for “scaled operations”

⚠️ Important Considerations

While the one-person company business model is advanced in the AI agent era, it also has vulnerabilities. Over-reliance on AI tools, lack of backup mechanisms, and data security risks require advance planning.

💰 Case Studies: AI Wealth Stories from Ordinary Entrepreneurs

For the past two decades, tech industry wealth stories were almost always tied to elite university dropouts, Silicon Valley garages, and VC backing. But in the last two years, a group of ordinary people who can’t code, lack prestigious credentials, and have never raised a dime in funding are quietly making hundreds of thousands to millions of dollars through AI.

What Did They Do Right?

  • Vibe Coding Model: No need to master programming - use AI tools to bring ideas to life
  • Rapid Validation: From idea to product launch in days or even hours
  • Small but Beautiful Strategy: Focus on niche needs, not pursuing “big and comprehensive”
  • Personal Leverage: Transform technical skills, writing ability, and investment experience into reusable, distributable, and sellable assets

1. Website Building Tools

2. R&D Resource Management Tools

💡 Practical Advice

For Those Wanting to Start a One-Person Company

  1. Start from Niche Needs: Don’t try to build a big platform - focus on a specific pain point
  2. Leverage AI Tool Multipliers: Use AI to amplify your capability boundaries
  3. Build Personal Brand: Content creation is the lowest-cost customer acquisition method
  4. Maintain Financial Health: Control costs, prioritize cash flow over fundraising
  5. Build Backup Mechanisms: Don’t over-rely on single tools or platforms

⚠️ Risk Warning
