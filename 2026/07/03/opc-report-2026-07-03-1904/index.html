I. OPC Model: Not Just “Solo Work”, But a Revolution in Organizational Logic

1.1 What is OPC?

OPC stands for One-Person Company. This concept started breaking into the mainstream from late 2025. Within months, governments from Shanghai Lingang to Beijing Zhongguancun, from Suzhou Industrial Park to Guangdong Foshan, have been competing to introduce support policies and build OPC-specific entrepreneurship zones.

📊 Key Data:

  • 2019: Percentage of new companies founded by a single founder: 23.7%
  • First Half of 2025: This percentage: 36.3%
  • Increase: Over 50%
  • Early 2026: Month-on-month growth rate of solo-founded AI startups in China: 22%

1.2 The Core Logic of OPC

The essence of the OPC model is not technology—it’s a complete reconstruction of organizational logic.

Traditional Company OPC Model
Hire people → Division of labor → Collaboration → Delivery Set goals → Deploy Agents → Auto-collaboration → Auto-delivery
More people = higher management costs, diminishing marginal efficiency Fewer people = clearer scheduling, linearly scalable efficiency

Professor Mao Ning from Nanjing University Business School provides the most succinct summary:

“OPC is essentially ‘one-person company + AI cluster’—individuals amplify their capabilities through AI agents, achieving ‘one person as an army’. One person + a suite of AI agents = a ‘company’ that requires no commuting, is emotionally stable, has explosive efficiency, and operates 7×24 hours.”

1.3 The OPC Formula

Carbon-based Intelligence (Human) + Silicon-based Execution (AI) = Complete Company

4.1 Trend 1: From “AI-Assisted” to “AI-Native”

The business underlying architecture of OPC will be fully AI-ized. It’s no longer “human + tools,” but “human commanding AI cluster.”

  • Marketing: AI fully automated content matrix (articles + videos + social)

⚠️ Risk Warning

  1. OPC is not “easy money”—it requires extreme self-discipline and learning ability
  2. AI tools iterate rapidly—you need to continuously keep up with new technologies
  3. Income is unstable—you need to have sufficient reserves
  4. Strong sense of isolation—you need good psychological resilience
  5. Invest with caution, don’t blindly follow trends!

🎯 Advice for OPC Entrepreneurs

  1. Run one closed loop first: Don’t bite off more than you can chew—make a profitable MVP first
  2. Leverage AI tools well: Hand all repetitive work to AI—you only make decisions
  3. Continuous learning: AI tools iterate fast—maintain curiosity and learning ability
  4. Join communities: OPC entrepreneurs empower each other—don’t fight alone
  5. Compliant operation: A one-person company is still a company—tax/legal affairs cannot be ignored
