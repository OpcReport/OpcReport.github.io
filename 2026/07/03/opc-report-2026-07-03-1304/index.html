1. OPC Definition and Core Philosophy

1.1 What is OPC?

OPC (One-Person Company) is a new entrepreneurship model with the following core characteristics:

Dimension Description Counter-Example
👤 Solo Operation Built and maintained by 1 person (or at most 5-person team) ❌ Projects requiring large-scale teams
💵 Sustainable Revenue Has stable cash flow (MRR/ARR) ❌ Pure open-source projects/hobby projects
🤖 Leverage Effect Relies on software/content/automation, not manpower ❌ Consulting companies/labor-intensive services
🎨 Autonomous Control Pursues freedom and autonomy, not blind expansion ❌ VC-driven high-growth companies
💡 Core Philosophy Build a life, not just a company

1.2 Why Did OPC Suddenly Explode in 2026?

  • AI Tool Maturation: 2024-2026 AI Agent and automation tool chains achieved breakthroughs; individuals can now dispatch “thousands of troops”
  • Lowered Technical Barriers: Shifted from “can you write Prompts” to “can you deliver Agent products”
  • Market Demand Changes: Enterprises need vertical, flexible, and efficient solutions rather than bloated traditional SaaS
  • Policy Support: 20+ cities in China have introduced OPC-specific support policies; Hainan Free Trade Port offers up to 8 million RMB in rewards

8. OPC Entrepreneurship Success Methodology

8.1 30-Day OPC Launch Checklist

Week 1: Validate Ideas

  • Identify target user pain points (interview at least 10 people)
  • Find related complaints on Reddit/X/Xiaohongshu
  • Create simple landing page (Carrd/Framer)
  • Pre-sell: Try to get first paid users before product development

Week 2: Rapidly Develop MVP

  • Use AI tools to accelerate development (GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Claude)
  • Focus on core features, cut non-essential features
  • “Launch if it runs, fail fast, iterate continuously”

Week 3: Launch and Promotion

  • Deploy to Vercel/Supabase and other platforms
  • Publish on Product Hunt, Indie Hackers, Xiaohongshu, and other platforms
  • Collect user feedback, iterate rapidly

Week 4: Commercialization and Growth

  • Integrate payment system (Stripe/PayPal/WeChat Pay)
  • Set up subscription model (MRR)
  • Content marketing: Write technical blogs, record tutorial videos

8.2 OPC Success Core Principles

⚠️ Common Misconceptions

  • Misconception 1: “One person does everything” → Correct Approach: One person learns to design task flows, then uses AI Agent, automation tools, and external service networks to run tasks
  • Misconception 2: “Pursue perfection before launch” → Correct Approach: “Launch if it runs, fail fast, iterate continuously”
  • Misconception 3: “Blindly expand team” → Correct Approach: Keep it small and beautiful, pursue sustainable revenue not blind expansion

✅ Success Keys

  • Leverage Thinking: AI tools have shifted from auxiliary roles to core productivity units that are programmable, collaborative, and can undertake systematic tasks
  • Task Flow Design: Not one person stubbornly handling product, R&D, sales, customer service, operations, delivery, but designing task flows
  • Rapid Validation: 30-day launch, rapid idea validation, avoid working behind closed doors
  • Community-Driven: Utilize Indie Hackers, Reddit, X, and other platforms to get feedback and resources

  • Long-term Memory: AI Agents possess long-term memory capability, can continuously improve workflows

10.3 Challenges and Risks

⚠️ Challenges to Note

  • AI Hallucination Problem: AI-generated content may have factual errors, requires manual review
  • Homogenization Competition: Lowered OPC barriers lead to massive similar products, need differentiation
  • Compliance Risks: AI-generated content copyright, data privacy and other compliance issues need attention
  • Continuous Learning Pressure: Rapid AI tool iteration speed requires continuous learning of new tools

11. Conclusions and Recommendations

🎯 Core Conclusion
OPC (One-Person Company) has shifted from concept to reality in 2026. The maturation of AI tools has made “one person + AI = one company” possible. China’s 16 million one-person companies and the US’s 30.4 million one-person companies are reshaping the business ecosystem. OPC is not “one person does everything”, but “one person learns to design task flows, then uses AI Agent, automation tools, and external service networks to run tasks”.

11.1 Recommendations for OPC Entrepreneurs

  1. Launch Quickly: Don’t pursue perfection, launch if it runs, fail fast, iterate continuously
  2. Focus on Vertical: Choose vertical fields you’re familiar with, cultivate deeply
  3. Leverage AI: Delegate repetitive work to AI, focus on creativity and decision-making yourself
  4. Community-Driven: Actively participate in Indie Hackers, Reddit, X, and other communities, get feedback and resources
  5. Compliant Operations: Pay attention to AI-generated content copyright, data privacy and other compliance issues
  6. Continuous Optimization: Improve products based on user feedback, pursue MRR growth not blind expansion

11.2 Recommendations for Policy Makers

  1. Increase Support: More cities introduce OPC-specific support policies (workspace subsidies, computing power subsidies, tax preferences)
  2. Build OPC Parks: Create OPC exclusive entrepreneurship parks, provide free workspaces + computing power subsidies + training services
  3. Host OPC Competitions: Discover potential projects through competitions, link investment institutions and industrial resources
  4. Improve Laws and Regulations: Clarify legal issues such as AI-generated content copyright and data privacy

11.3 Recommendations for Investment Institutions

  1. Focus on OPC Track: OPC project financing explosion, expected to break 20 billion RMB in second half of 2026
  2. Emphasize MRR Metrics: Core value of OPC projects lies in sustainable revenue, not user scale
  3. Invest Early and Small: OPC project launch costs are low (90% of independent developers start with less than 500 USD), early investment return space is large
