⚠️ OPC Entrepreneurship Guide: Three Core Prerequisites

Reduce Failure Risk Considerations

  1. Low-Cost Startup

    • No need to invest large amounts of money in office space rental, employee recruitment
    • Relying on AI tools, 10,000-20,000 yuan (or even less) can start
    • Main costs: AI tool subscription, basic materials, or skill learning

  2. Align with Own Advantages

    • No need to pursue “hot tracks,” prioritize directions related to your own professional experience and skills
    • Reduce learning costs, improve success rate
    • Example: Administrative professionals can do AI-assisted office consulting, designers can do AI visual customization

  3. Choose Suitable Tools

    • Cost-Effectiveness: Prioritize tools with free tiers to reduce trial-and-error costs
    • Learning Curve: Choose products with complete documentation and active communities, quickly find solutions when encountering problems
    • Scenario Matching: Choose professional tools for vertical domains based on your own business needs, rather than pursuing all-encompassing tools

OPC Vulnerabilities: Risk Control

  • Single-Point Failure Risk: OPCs lack redundancy; founder health issues or technical failures may cause business interruption
  • Weak Stress Resistance: Facing unexpected events (e.g., platform policy changes, supply chain interruptions), OPCs have limited response capability
  • Knowledge Island: All business knowledge is concentrated in the founder alone, difficult to inherit and expand

Recommended Countermeasures:

  • Establish automated backups and emergency plans
  • Purchase relevant insurance (e.g., income protection insurance)
  • Gradually establish knowledge base, make tacit knowledge explicit
  • Consider establishing cooperative relationships with OPCs having complementary skills
