5. Methodology: 90 Days to Your First Cash Flow

5.1 Right order: validate a “billable problem” first

The 2026 OPC Survival Guide warns: don’t register a company, lease an office, or build a big system up front. Correct sequence — find a problem customers will pay for, sell a service before a standardized product, and validate one small loop (acquire → close → deliver → retain) before scaling. Delegate “roles” to AI: research, content, sales, support, ops each mapped to an Agent; the founder orchestrates, not executes.

5.2 Seven plays & seven lean-asset domains
