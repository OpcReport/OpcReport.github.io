⚠️ Risk Warnings & Cold Reflections

Under the OPC boom, three major risks deserve vigilance:

  1. Policy Dependency Risk: Subsidies are catalysts, not life-saving medicine. Your business model must independently sustain — those still standing after subsidies fade are the real winners.
