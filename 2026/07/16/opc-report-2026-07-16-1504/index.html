MIIT + 7 Departments Accelerate AI One-Person Company Cultivation

China’s Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, along with six other departments, issued a joint policy directive in June 2026 formally accelerating the cultivation of AI OPCs (One-Person Companies) — marking the AI one-person-company model as a national strategic initiative, with over 20 cities now having dedicated OPC support policies.

Policy & Ecosystem

  • China’s MIIT + 7 Departments: AI OPC Policy Directive (Jun 2026) — Shanghai, Jiangsu, Guangdong and other provinces have integrated OPC into their 15th Five-Year Plans. Over 20 Chinese cities have now issued OPC-specific incentive policies, shifting the model from individual experimentation to national strategic layout. (Source: QQ/企鹅号, 2026-07-06)
  • Shenzhen: AI OPC Action Plan (2026–2027) — The city released a dedicated action plan targeting 10+ OPC communities, 1,000+ AI startups, and 10,000+ AI entrepreneurship talents by end of 2027. OPC communities nationwide are evolving from co-working spaces into hybrid organizations connecting AI tools, solo founders, industrial resources, and enterprise application scenarios. (Source: CSDN, 2026-07-13)
  • WAIC + OpenCSG: World’s First OPC Independent Pioneer Challenge — The World AI Conference and OpenCSG launched the inaugural OPC Independent Pioneer Challenge, a premier incubation platform for super-individuals and AI entrepreneurs. Sam Altman’s prediction that “one person can build a $10B unicorn in the AI era” is gaining traction — data shows over one-third of new companies are now founded by solo founders, a 53% surge over six years. (Source: CSDN/OpenCSG, 2026-07-14)
  • Hainan Free Trade Port: OPC Incentive Policy Effective Jun 20, 2026 — Hainan Province’s four departments jointly issued a专项扶持 policy valid through 2028, stacking benefits across taxation, cross-border, and workspace categories. Up to ¥8M one-time reward for 1–3 quality OPC eco-communities per year, with additional personal entrepreneur subsidies. (Source: 博客园, 2026-06-07)

AI Tools & Technology Stack

  • Skills Abstraction Layer: AI Agents’ Leap from Tool Caller to Task Executor — Skills packages atomic tools into reusable capability units, reducing agent cognitive load from “managing 100 tool combinations” to “selecting 10 skills to complete a goal.” Solopreneurs in 2026 can rapidly build complete systems via Skills abstraction layers, enabling natural-language task orchestration without traditional coding. (Source: CSDN, 2026-05-04)
  • Realforce Intelligence “Lobster Matrix”: AI Employee Solution for One-Person Companies — With OpenClaw and other open-source agent frameworks maturing, AI has evolved from “Q&A assistant” to autonomous planning and execution agent. Realforce Intelligence launched the “Lobster Matrix” solution, allowing entrepreneurs to command a team of AI agents as a “digital workforce” for end-to-end commercial operations from idea to delivery. (Source: CSDN, 2026-04-21)
  • 2026 One-Person Company AI Tool Stack: 10 AI Super-Employees + 36 Tools — Emerging standard tool matrix for OPCs: Strategy layer (Notion, PerplexityAI, NotebookLM), Content layer (ChatGPT, Claude, DeepSeek), Visual layer (Midjourney, JIMENG AI), Video layer (AI video generation tools), Development layer (AI coding tools). One person can now accomplish what previously required an entire team. (Source: CSDN, 2026-05-08)
  • GitHub: awesome-ai-tools Curated List Updated 2026 — A comprehensive curated list of AI tools across LLMs, video, audio, AI agents, automation, developer platforms, and infrastructure, actively maintained and covering the most relevant systems for solopreneurs building in 2026. (Source: GitHub/eudk/awesome-ai-tools, 2026-06-26)
  • SourceForge: Agentic AI Tools for Android — AnyClaw, Viktor, Chatbox — AnyClaw (Android-based OpenClaw + Codex + Claude Code), Viktor (AI agent connecting 3,000+ tools), and Chatbox (cross-platform AI client with Ollama support) represent the mobile-first AI tooling landscape for indie makers and solopreneurs. (Source: SourceForge, 2026-06-12)

Startup Cases & Market Data

  • Swedish Lovable: $100M ARR in 8 Months, $6.6B Valuation — When Sweden’s Lovable reached $100M annualized revenue in just 8 months with a $6.6B valuation; when Israel’s Base44 (8-person team, 90% AI-generated code) hit $1.5M ARR in 3 weeks before being acquired for $80M — AI OPC has moved from concept to scaled implementation, becoming the most-watched tech startup trend of 2026. (Source: 搜狐, 2026-05-31)
  • AI-Powered Short Drama, Virtual Idol, Music Creation: Solo Entrepreneurship Expanding — AI tools are disrupting traditional content production: solo creators completing 88-minute AI micro-dramas at under ¥10,000 cost; solo virtual idol agencies reaching 1M+ overseas followers; music demo production compressed from one week to five minutes with ¥1.5M annual revenue projections; voice cloning costs reduced to 0.1% of traditional methods. (Source: 搜狐, 2026-04-28)
  • Programmer Wolf’s OPC Practice: goFly.v1kf.com Solo AI Agent Model — A Chinese developer built an OPC model around “solo + AI agents,” achieving end-to-end commercial operations from R&D to deployment to multi-platform management. The self-built product gofly.v1kf.com exemplifies the “core competencies internalized, non-core outsourced” OPC philosophy. (Source: 博客园, 2026-05-11)
  • Indie Maker Atta: Building AI-Powered Tools in Public — Solo maker Atta publishes regular build logs on attacomsian.com, showcasing AI-powered tool development workflow, rapid shipping, and small-bet product strategy for indie makers building in 2026. (Source: attacomsian.com, 2026-07-15)
  • AI Agent Economic Value: 3–5 Full-Time Employees’ Workload at Under 10% of Labor Cost — 2026 labeled the “Digital Employee Year Zero.” AI agents handle daily task volumes equivalent to 3–5 full-time employees at under 10% of traditional labor costs. Using OpenClaw, Miaoda, and similar AI agents, entrepreneurs are becoming “super individuals” capable of independently building commercial-grade platforms in vertical domains: AI web series, smart wearables, AI+HR, and enterprise intelligent sites. (Source: CSDN, 2026-07-13)

Solo founders were well-represented among the cohort. (Source: 企鹅号 via YC coverage, 2026-03-27)
  • TechCrunch: AI Race Shifts to Enterprise Agents — TechCrunch coverage highlights the evolving AI competitive landscape, with major funding rounds, agent-focused product launches, and shifting focus from frontier models to practical enterprise and solo-use AI agent deployment. (Source: TechCrunch, 2026-07-15)
  • gosolo.org: Solopreneur Resources and Community — Updated 2026 solopreneur resource hub offering business startup checklists, tool recommendations, and community support for launching side-hustle and passive income businesses as solopreneurs. (Source: gosolo.org, 2026-07-15)
  • Solopreneur OS (Skillhub): Workflow Refactoring for AI-Assisted Solo Operations — Emerging “one-person company operating system” frameworks are standardizing the approach to decomposing complex workflows into AI-assisted solo execution, bridging the gap between individual capability and company-scale output.
