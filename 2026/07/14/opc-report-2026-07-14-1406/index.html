⚠️ Risk Alerts for Solo Founders

The Efficiency Trap

Warning from Kai-Fu Lee (01.AI Founder): Over the past two years, enterprise AI has clustered entirely in the execution layer, falling into a serious “efficiency trap.” Simply improving operational efficiency doesn’t change a company’s fundamental business metrics. Enterprise AI needs to reach the decision-making table.

Translation for Solo Founders: Don’t just use AI to do the same things faster. Use AI to do things differently—make better decisions, identify new opportunities, create novel value.

Other Risk Factors:

  1. AI Siphon Effect: DRAM and NAND flash memory supply tightening, hardware costs rising
  2. Tool Fragmentation: Mainstream AI creation platforms have obvious capability gaps, requiring multiple software switches
  3. Competition Intensification: Everyone can build products now, differentiation threshold is higher
