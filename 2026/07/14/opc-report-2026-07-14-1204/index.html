3. Trend Outlook

① From “using AI” to “orchestrating task flows.”
The truly competitive OPC in 2026 is not one person shouldering everything, but someone who decomposes business into task flows and runs them with AI Agents, automation, and external service networks. AI has, for the first time, significantly enlarged the individual’s “manageable radius.”

② City competition enters the “ecosystem operation” stage.
Support has upgraded from desks and subsidies to compute, scenarios, expert coaching, and capital matchmaking. OPC communities are becoming the new “industrial park 2.0.”

③ Bubbles and polarization coexist — beware the “effortless win” narrative.
Some communities hit RMB 5M in sales in 4 months; others lost everything. OPC is not risk-free, and “solo unicorn” is an extreme, not a median. Compliance, taxation, and IP protection remain concerns.
