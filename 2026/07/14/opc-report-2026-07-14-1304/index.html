🔧 OPC Operating System: Workflow Reconstruction Methodology

Core Philosophy: The true essence of OPC is not “using AI to replace humans in traditional workflows,” but reconstructing workflows based on AI capability models.

The Reconstruction Principle

Traditional Workflow Complexity = Task Complexity + Human Limitation Compensation Layer

  • Human brain capacity is limited → tasks get split into stages
  • Document handoffs between people → intermediate artifacts created
  • Collaboration friction → management coordination required

OPC Workflow Design Rules:

  1. Use IPO primitive chains instead of human decision nodes
  2. Eliminate “human limitation compensation layers”
  3. Execution axis = 6-dimensional capability calls, not role assignments

IPO Primitive Chain Model

Guidelines:

  • If a task can be expressed as an IPO chain, don’t add human intervention
  • Break complex tasks into simple IPO primitives
  • Chain primitives together for sophisticated workflows
