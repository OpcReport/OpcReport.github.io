From central to local governments, OPC support policies are landing rapidly. The new Company Law removes the “one natural person, one OPC” restriction. Small micro-enterprises pay an effective ~5% tax rate on profits under ¥3M. Shenzhen, Shanghai, Hangzhou and others offer dedicated OPC service windows, free coworking, and computing vouchers. The policy landscape provides a strong institutional foundation for OPC growth.

2. Mature AI Toolchain Creates “Super Individuals”

  • Vibe Coding market: $4.7B
  • 41% of global code written by AI
  • 92% of developers use AI coding tools daily
  • Individual developers can deliver full-stack products using Claude Code, Cursor, etc.
  • AI agents evolve from “chat assistants” to “digital employees” — programmable, collaborative core productivity units

3. Competitive Landscape Reshaping

While AI lowers entry barriers, it also intensifies competition. Everyone has an AI assistant now, meaning the number of entrants skyrockets. Experts advise OPC entrepreneurs to think beyond tool arbitrage — build differentiated value around personal brand/IP, industry know-how, client trust, and proprietary data.

4. Proven Commercialization Paths

SaaS subscriptions, knowledge monetization, API services, AI deployment services, content monetization — all validated models. Gross margins reaching 98% on lightweight operations are attracting more entrants. OPC is no longer just “freelancing 2.0” — it’s becoming a parallel business ecosystem alongside traditional SMEs.
