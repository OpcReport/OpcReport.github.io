3. Trend Analysis

  • From “information-arbitrage dividend” to “execution-arbitrage dividend”: everyone now knows AI is powerful; the scarce resource is those who can wire AI into real business workflows. Prompt engineering and Agent orchestration become core competitiveness.
  • Organization cost is being repriced: what took 3–5 people now runs with 1 person + models + automation; the startup threshold drops from “organizational” to “personal.”
  • “Sell services first, then standardize into products”: services are the fastest cash-flow and demand validator; expand only after one small loop (acquire → close → deliver → repurchase) is proven.
  • Capital is starting to pay for OPC: China OPC equity financing +400% YoY; Lovable and Base44 redefine the “small team, high ARR” valuation logic.
  • Policy and infrastructure are maturing: compute vouchers, talent subsidies, and a simplified Company Law — combined with mature digital payments and logistics — form China’s unique soil for OPC scale adoption.

Risk Note

OPC does not mean “zero-risk passive income.” Agent hallucination, compliance boundaries, customer privacy, and revenue volatility remain real challenges. Some self-media overhype “easy money for beginners.” Founders should rely on real cash flow and verifiable metrics, and evaluate prudently.
