7. Cold Truth & Risks

⚠️ Not every OPC makes money. Research shows profitable OPCs cluster in three types: ① executors who wire AI into real business flows; ② “expert” individuals in extremely vertical niches; ③ people who use AI to amplify their ability, not chase a fantasy “money printer.” AI is a lever, not a printer — it amplifies your strength and your blind spots.

  • Compute/Token is a hidden cost — running 17 agents racks up a real token bill; unit economics must be calculated.
  • Platform-dependency risk — multi-platform side hustles are directly exposed to platform audits, throttling, and rule changes.
  • “No-human company” compliance gap — purely agent-autonomous content/transactions remain a gray zone on liability, tax, and consumer rights.
