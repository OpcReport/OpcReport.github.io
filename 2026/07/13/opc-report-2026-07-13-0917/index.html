🔬 Deep Dive: Why 2026 Is the Last OPC Dividend Period?

Signal 1: Policy Foundation — Trial Cost Drops to Freezing Point

New Company Law fully deregulates. One person can now establish “subsidiary one-person companies,” completely unlocking capital fission chains. Effective tax rate drops to 5% on profits under ¥3M. The state is building an extremely wide moat to protect super individuals. But this policy window will not stay open forever.

Signal 2: Productivity Leverage — One Person = An Army

AI tools have upgraded from “assistant role” to core productivity unit. Technical execution is no longer the barrier — commercial judgment and integration capability are. 92% of developers use AI daily; 41% of all code is AI-generated. This means the “technical barrier” is dissolving at visible speed.

Signal 3: City Competition — Subsidy Window Is Limited

Shenzhen, Shanghai, Suzhou, Chengdu, Hangzhou are in an all-out subsidy race: free rent, compute vouchers, loan discounts… This is not normal — it’s short-term city warfare for talent. As the OPC ecosystem matures, policy perks will eventually exit.

Signal 4: First-Mover Advantage Is Accumulating

Shanghai Lingang: someone hit ¥5M in 4 months. Zhongguancun: 32 OPCs already forming a community. First-movers are already accumulating brand equity, credit, and user assets. The later you enter, the smaller the opportunity window.

⚠️ Risk Reminder

OPC is NOT suitable for everyone. After reviewing hundreds of cases, only three types consistently profit:

  1. Tech wizards with decisive competitive advantages
  2. Niche market players who accurately position in underserved segments
  3. Service OPCs who dramatically improve efficiency via AI

Blindly entering and losing everything is equally real. “Invest wisely — don’t just follow me into trades!”
