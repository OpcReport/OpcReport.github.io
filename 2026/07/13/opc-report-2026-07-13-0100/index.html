Track 5: AI Hardware & Vertical Applications

At CES 2026, Chinese companies became the focus, opening the “Physical AI” era. The combination of AI and hardware has become a new trend, with individual entrepreneurs able to enter specific vertical domains.

Track 6: Education/Knowledge Monetization

The core principle is: build once, sell many times. When products can be automatically delivered, automatically renewed, and automatically supported, founders transition from “trading time for money” to “trading systems for money.”
