China Launches First National AI OPC Policy as WAIC 2026 Opens Tomorrow

China’s Yunnan Province has issued the nation’s first dedicated AI OPC support policy while the Greater Bay Area unveils the first public cloud-based AI OPC community at WAIC 2026, marking a new phase in national-level one-person company support.

  • Yunnan Provincial Human Resources Dept · 2026-07-16: Issued “Several Measures on Supporting AI OPC Entrepreneurship,” covering four dimensions (carriers, funding, talent, services) with nine specific policy items. Yunnan OPC is currently in an early-stage rapid ramp-up phase with insufficient talent supply and incomplete policy support systems.
  • Shenzhen News / GBA Big Data Center · 2026-07-16: The first nationwide public-benefit cloud-based AI OPC community will launch at WAIC 2026 (July 17–20), serving small and micro entrepreneurs across the Greater Bay Area.
  • Chengdu High-Tech Zone / CCTV · 2026-07-14: China’s first Tencent WorkBuddy OPC Cooperative Community officially launched at Tianfu Long Island Digital Creative Park, jointly initiated by Tencent Cloud and Chengdu Hi-Tech Cultural & Creative Media. The community uses Tencent’s proprietary AI workbench WorkBuddy with OPC as the core organizational model.
  • China News Service / Yangzhou · 2026-07-16: Yangzhou held a network united front initiative to empower AI OPC innovation and entrepreneurship, announcing a goal to build “China’s Best OPC Entrepreneurship City.”
  • CSDN / BaiRong Intelligence · 2026-07-16: BaiRong Intelligence will debut four “Silicon-Based Employee” products at WAIC 2026, having already deployed over 200,000 silicon-based employees serving 8,000+ institutional clients — the company signals AI has moved beyond “can chat” into the “can do” era.

  • CSDN · 2026-07-15: Shenzhen “Model Power Camp” AI ecosystem hosts ~200 OPC companies; Shanghai Lingang Zero Boundary Magic Cube’s 500+ workstations filled rapidly. Guangdong spring recruitment fair first to establish a “Super Individual” dedicated zone.
