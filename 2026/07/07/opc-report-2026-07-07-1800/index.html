🔥 Weekly Hot Topics

1️⃣ Policy Benefits Continue to Expand

Shenzhen takes the lead with the “Shenzhen AI OPC Entrepreneurship Ecosystem Leadership Action Plan (2026-2027)”, aiming to build 10+ OPC communities and attract 10,000+ AI entrepreneurship talents by end of 2027. Cities like Wuhan and Chengdu have also introduced support policies, incorporating OPC into their AI entrepreneurship ecosystems.

2️⃣ AI-Native Platforms Spark SaaS Disruption

AI-native cloud platforms like OpenClaw continue iterating, enabling solo developers to complete full-stack development, product operations, and customer service with AI assistance alone. The S&P North America Software Index dropped 15% in a single month, and DocuSign’s annual decline approaches 30%, signaling traditional SaaS foundations are crumbling.

3️⃣ OPC Independent Pioneer Challenge Officially Launched

The OPC Independent Pioneer Challenge, jointly launched by WAIC and OpenCSG, has become the world’s first top-tier incubation platform exclusively for super individuals and AI entrepreneurs. Adopts strict three-round progression: Online Preliminary → City Semi-finals → WAIC Finals.

4️⃣ Bika.ai Launches OPC-Specific AI Tool Suite

On July 6, Bika.ai officially released an AI tool platform specifically designed for one-person companies, supporting multi-agent collaborative chat, workflow customization, and helping super individuals build their own AI teams.
