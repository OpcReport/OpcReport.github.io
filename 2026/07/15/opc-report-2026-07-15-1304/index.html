Don’t be swept by the myth: Insiders state plainly that “a one-person AI company earning ¥2M/month” is real money but absolutely not earned lying down — you earn the “architect’s” and “supervisor’s” money, not the “laborer’s”. Survivorship bias is real: winners are amplified, failed projects stay silent.

  • Survivorship bias: Public cases are mostly “¥100K+/month” winners; the silent majority is still iterating. Solo operation means all risk concentrates on one person.
