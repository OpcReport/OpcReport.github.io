For super-individuals running “multiple AI-powered business lines,” this is both opportunity and a compliance red line.

6. Risks & Traps (Red Alert)

  • Homogeneous competition: With tech barriers near zero, pure “AI content / wrapped tools” have shallow moats and are easily cloned in bulk.
  • Engineering loss of control: Small teams “hand-rolling Agents” look low-barrier, but with fuzzy SOPs they easily spiral into stack chaos and abandoned projects.
  • Compliance debt: Under the new Company Law, sole-person companies bear reversed burden of proof on property separation — mixing personal and corporate = joint liability.
  • Platform policy risk: Traffic and rules dependent on a single platform (TikTok/Shopify) can go to zero overnight on policy change.
  • “Wild” to “super-individual” gap: Wrong tool selection doubles dev cycles; fuzzy personal-brand positioning bleeds followers.
