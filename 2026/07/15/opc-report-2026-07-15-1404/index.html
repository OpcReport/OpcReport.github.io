🚀 Major Policy Breakthroughs

1. National Competition Opens Track for “One-Person Companies”

On July 3, 2026, the National AIGC Multimodal Innovation Design and Technology Application Competition (AI OPC Group) launched in Beijing. This marks the first time a national-level competition platform has opened an independent track for “one-person companies.”

Guided by: Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Education, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, China Association for Science and Technology

The organizer, Jinshang AI OPC Entrepreneurship Incubation Platform, simultaneously announced the “Million AI OPC Empowerment Project” to drive employment through entrepreneurship and promote the large-scale development of the OPC model.

2. Hong Kong: First OPC Innovation Hub Launches at Cyberport

Cyberport officially launched Hong Kong’s first innovation and entrepreneurship base specifically designed for “one-person companies” - OPC Hub, and simultaneously established the “Web4.0 and Agent Security Alliance.”

OPC Hub’s one-stop support includes:

  • AI inference and computing power resources
  • Model usage quotas
  • Cloud services and network support
  • Shared workspace
  • Investor matchmaking
  • Industry connection resources

3. Zhejiang Province: 100+ OPC Communities by 2028

The first “AI+OPC” Innovation and Development Conference was held in Hangzhou, explicitly setting the ambitious goal of building 100+ OPC communities by 2028. Liu Jie, Deputy Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Party Committee and Governor, personally visited the Hangzhou “Honghuhui” OPC community for face-to-face exchanges with frontline entrepreneurs.

4. Hubei Province: Special Policy Support for AI OPC

The Hubei Provincial People’s Government Office issued the “Several Measures to Support the Development of AI OPC in Hubei Province (Trial)”, supporting OPC in conducting technology innovation R&D, vertical scenario applications, and industrial ecosystem services, promoting deep integration of technological innovation, industrial innovation, and financial innovation.
