2026 H2 OPC One-Person Company AI Startup Trend Report: Policy Breakthrough, Tool Empowerment and the Rise of Solo Entrepreneurs

2026 H2 OPC One-Person Company AI Startup Trend Report: Policy Breakthrough, Tool Empowerment and the Rise of Solo Entrepreneurs

Report Generation Time: July 4, 2026 18:23 | Monitoring Scope: June 2026 to Present

1. Policy Dividend Release: National and Local Levels Increase OPC Support

2026 has become the first year for OPC (One-Person Company) entrepreneurship to gain official recognition. National and local authorities have intensively introduced support policies to remove institutional barriers for solo entrepreneurs:

  • National top-level design implementation: On June 18, seven departments including the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology jointly released the Action Plan for Promoting the Coordinated Development of Large, Medium and Small Enterprises in the Platform Economy (2026-2028), clearly proposing to “accelerate the cultivation of AI one-person companies” and incorporating the OPC model into the national entrepreneurship support system.
  • Local pilot ecosystem first landing: Beijing Haidian District, as the OPC innovation pilot area, appeared at the 2026 Digital Economy Industry Expo with more than 30 OPC entrepreneurship representatives. According to the overall design of “overall cognition, ecological elements, policy direct access, mechanism guarantee, and space platform”, it demonstrated the “super individual” innovation ecosystem for the AI era. Some OPC projects have obtained special policy subsidies and resource docking support.

2. Entrepreneurship Paradigm Revolution: OPC Moves from Concept to Large-Scale Implementation

The maturity of AI technology has turned “one person is a company” from a startup slogan into a landing business model. Industry data has verified the explosive power of this trend:

▶ Global startup data shows that more than one-third of new companies are founded by solo founders, and this proportion has soared by 53% in six years. Solo entrepreneurship has become one of the mainstream entrepreneurial forms.
▶ The prophecy of OpenAI CEO Sam Altman is coming true: “In the AI era, one person may be able to found a unicorn company with a valuation of $1 billion.” At present, several AI tool projects developed by solo founders have exceeded $100 million in valuation.

In the traditional entrepreneurship model, small and medium-sized developers face high thresholds and long processes to integrate into large enterprise systems. In the OPC system, individuals or small teams can independently complete the whole process of product design, operation promotion, and commercial realization. Creative and technical capabilities have become the core access standards, completely breaking the resource barriers for entrepreneurship.

3. Full-Link Tool Matrix: AI Covers the Whole Process of OPC from 0 to 1

The maturity of AI tools in 2026 has covered the whole process of OPC entrepreneurship, and a single person can complete the work that used to require dozens of teams:

3.1 Market Research and Product Design Stage

GPTs with web search and analysis capabilities, special analysis agents can automatically complete industry research, competitor analysis, and user demand mining, and output professional market analysis reports, with a cost only 1/10 of traditional research.

3.2 Product Development and Engineering Stage

AI programming assistants such as GitHub Copilot, Cursor, and Claude Code can complete 80% of code writing and debugging work. Combined with low-code platforms such as Retool and FlutterFlow, a single person can complete the development and launch of MVP (Minimum Viable Product) within 1-2 weeks, and the development efficiency is increased by 5-10 times compared with the traditional model.

3.3 Commercialization and Operation Stage

AI marketing tools can automatically complete content generation, channel delivery, and user conversion; AI customer service tools can respond to user needs 7*24 hours; SaaS tools such as payment, finance, taxation, and legal affairs can automatically complete compliance processes, and a single person can support the large-scale operation of tens of thousands of users.

3.4 Vertical Service Tools Emerge

Moxun Technology has launched a special tool for SoloFounder OPC to help freelancers and slash youths transform personal skills into standardized and replicable commercial products, solving the pain point of “having skills but no realization path”. It has served more than 20,000 users in the three months since its launch.

4. High-Potential Track Inventory: These Fields Are Most Suitable for OPC to Break Through

Combined with the successful OPC entrepreneurship cases in the first half of 2026, the following tracks have become high-incidence areas for one-person companies:

  • AIGC tool development: For example, Su Kui, the founder of Nezha Huyu, developed the “Dragon Bone Animation” platform alone, which can generate 2D bone animation with one sentence, and recently won the first prize of the “Entrepreneurship Beijing” OPC characteristic competition, with revenue exceeding 1 million yuan in the first half year of launch.
  • Vertical AI applications: For example, the “AI sleep aiding companion” launched by Lingzhou Technology covers the whole sleep cycle, and builds a software and hardware content ecosystem for creators to directly reach users through AIGC sleep aiding music and health monitoring. The solo team’s annual revenue exceeds 10 million yuan.
  • Cross-border live streaming and independent station operation: AI translation, AI live streaming assistants, and automatic customer service tools enable a single person to operate multi-language live streaming rooms and cross-border independent stations. Some OPC teams have exceeded $100,000 in monthly cross-border revenue.
  • Vertical knowledge services: AI-assisted content generation, course production, and user operation enable solo knowledge bloggers to serve tens of thousands of paying users. The annual revenue of top OPC knowledge bloggers exceeds 10 million yuan.

5. Benchmark Cases: Pioneers Have Run Through the Commercialization Path

Case 1: Nezha Huyu – Solo Developed Animation Generation Tool, Won the Provincial Entrepreneurship Competition First Prize

Founder Su Kui completed all the development work of the “Dragon Bone Animation” platform alone. Users only need to input a sentence description to generate professional 2D bone animation, which greatly reduces the threshold of animation production. The project has accumulated more than 100,000 users in the first half year of launch, with revenue exceeding 1 million yuan. Recently, it won the first prize of the “Entrepreneurship Beijing” OPC characteristic competition and obtained 500,000 yuan interest-free loan and free office space support.

Case 2: Lingzhou Technology – Solo Built AI Health Hardware, Constructing Software and Hardware Content Ecosystem

The founder completed the hardware R&D, software algorithm optimization, and content ecosystem construction of the “AI sleep aiding companion” alone. The product covers the whole sleep cycle, and realizes the full life cycle value mining of users through AIGC sleep aiding music and health monitoring. At present, the monthly sales of the product exceed 10,000 units, the annual revenue exceeds 10 million yuan, and Series A financing has been launched.

Case 3: Humi Technology – Solo Team Cut into Vertical AI Enterprise Services, Achieved Stable Profit

Founder Chen Zishun completed the development and operation of AI marketing delivery tools alone, focusing on serving small and medium-sized e-commerce enterprises. The tools can automatically complete delivery strategy optimization, material generation, and effect analysis, with a customer renewal rate of more than 70%. The team has only one person, with annual revenue exceeding 5 million yuan and a net profit margin of more than 40%.

6. Trend Outlook: OPC Will Reconstruct the Future Entrepreneurship Ecosystem

2026 is only the first year of the OPC model outbreak. This model will profoundly reconstruct the entrepreneurship ecosystem in the next 3-5 years:

  • Entrepreneurship threshold will continue to decrease: With the further maturity of AI tools, the scope of work that can be completed by a single person will continue to expand. Creative and technical capabilities will completely replace capital and team size as the core access standards for entrepreneurship.
  • Organizational form will undergo fundamental changes: The traditional “company + employee” organizational form will gradually evolve into the “platform + super individual” form, and more people will choose to start businesses in the OPC model instead of entering large enterprises for employment.
  • Policy support will continue to increase: It is expected that more localities will introduce special OPC support policies in the future, including tax incentives, office space subsidies, green channels for financing, etc., to further reduce the institutional cost of OPC entrepreneurship.

For solo entrepreneurs who want to try OPC entrepreneurship, the second half of 2026 is the best time to enter: policy dividends are being released, the tool ecosystem has matured, and user demand is exploding. As long as you find the right vertical track, a single person can completely run out of large-scale commercial results.
