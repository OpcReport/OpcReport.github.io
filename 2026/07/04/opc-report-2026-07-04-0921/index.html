Executive Summary

The one-person company (OPC) model has evolved from a theoretical concept to a validated business paradigm in 2026. The critical insight: OPC success isn’t about one person doing everything—it’s about one person designing task flows and orchestrating AI Agents, automation tools, and external service networks to execute those tasks. AI has fundamentally expanded the “manageable scope” for individuals, transforming what previously required team building into solopreneur-powered micro-enterprises.
