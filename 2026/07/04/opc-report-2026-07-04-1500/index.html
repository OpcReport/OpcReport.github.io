📋 OPC Entrepreneurship Practical Guide

▎ Run Your First Cash Flow in 90 Days

Core Principle: Find problems first, then build products; sell services first, then create standardized products

Step 1 (Days 1-30): Validatable Billable Problem

  • Start from “problems customers will pay to solve” not “what I want to build”
  • Use AI for industry research and competitor analysis
  • Find 1 essential pain point in a niche sector

Step 2 (Days 31-60): Service Model Validation

  • Sell services first for fastest cash flow and demand validation
  • Use AI to mass-produce multi-platform content drafts
  • Build FAQ knowledge base + automated customer service

Step 3 (Days 61-90): Productization & Expansion

  • Run through 1 complete loop first, then expand
  • One loop contains: Acquisition → Transaction → Delivery → Repurchase
  • Standardize successful experience into digital products

▎ Common OPC Revenue Models

🎯 Industry Expert Insights

“OPC is not solo fighting, but one person with CEO mindset, integrating AI tools, outsourced collaboration, and platform resources, independently completing the full commercial loop from ‘demand insight → product delivery → revenue realization’.”

Different from freelancing: Freelancers ‘sell time for compensation’, getting paid once per task; OPC ‘sells systems for compound interest’, with capability models, customer networks, and delivery processes accumulating scale effects over time.

“You’re not doing all the work alone, but ‘one person coordinating multiple digital employees’.”

Key transformation: From “what I do” to “what I let AI do”. Assign research, content, sales, customer service, and operations roles to AI, while you focus on decision-making and optimization.
