Analysis & Actionable Takeaways

Risk note: OPC is not “zero-barrier passive income.” Multiple pieces this week point to engineering pitfalls (Agent projects easily stall, ambiguous SOP boundaries), platform-dependency risk (plugin-ecosystem policy shifts), and the reality that “AI flattens the tech barrier but amplifies the execution gap.”

  • The window is open, but the dividend is moving from “tools” to “scenarios.” Generic chatbots are severely commoditized; what actually monetizes is the “colleague who is ready to use” vertical Agent (legal, HR, restaurants, e-commerce).
  • Treat coding Agents as your core leverage. Tools like Claude Code / Codex can absorb large engineering loads; solo founders should spend time on “defining the problem + writing prompts + validating demand” rather than repetitive coding.
  • Rapidly prototype with a 3–5 day MVP. Prioritize platforms with existing payment and traffic infrastructure (Notion/Figma/Linear plugin markets, WeChat ecosystem) to cut acquisition and compliance cost, then consider standalone SaaS.
