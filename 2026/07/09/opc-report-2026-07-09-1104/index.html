OPC Trend Alert: AI Evolution from Tool to Decision-Maker

Report Date: July 9, 2026, 11:04 (Asia/Shanghai)
Keywords: One Person Company, AI Decision-Making, Agent Phone, Compliance, Super Individual

Executive Summary

July 2026 marks a critical turning point in the AI entrepreneurship ecosystem. Kai-Fu Lee launched “C-Suite Decision AI,” signaling AI’s evolution from execution tool to core decision-maker; Doubao and Qwen simultaneously discontinued user-built agent features as the industry enters a new compliance phase; Nubia unveiled the world’s first AI agent phone, providing strong hardware support for AI entrepreneurship.

1. Major Industry Events

🎯 Kai-Fu Lee Releases “C-Suite Decision AI”

On July 7, 01.AI founder Kai-Fu Lee launched three “C-Suite Decision AI” products at the Wance AI press conference: Boss AI, Sales Champion AI, and Investment Officer AI, along with the enterprise decision platform “Wance.”

Key Highlights:

  • Core Transformation: Enterprise AI value shifting from “improving execution efficiency” to “enhancing decision quality”
  • Technical Breakthrough: Inference costs dropped 175x over two years; top models now handle tasks requiring human experts’ 10+ hours
  • OPC Implications: Solo founders can access AI decision support, lowering strategic decision barriers

📱 World’s First AI Agent Phone Unveiling

ZTE subsidiary Nubia announced the world’s first AI agent phone, set to make its global debut at the 2026 World Artificial Intelligence Conference (July 17-20, Shanghai).

Key Points:

  • Hardware Innovation: AI agents deeply integrated into the operating system layer
  • Use Cases: Native hardware support for mobile AI entrepreneurship
  • Market Impact: May redefine mobile AI application ecosystem

⚖️ Doubao & Qwen Discontinue User-Built Agent Features

On July 4, ByteDance’s Doubao and Alibaba’s Qwen simultaneously announced terminating user-built agent services on July 15, aligning with the “Interim Measures for the Management of AI Anthropomorphic Interaction Services.”

Implications:

  • Compliance Requirement: Industry transitioning from wild growth to regulated development
  • Impact Scope: Higher barriers for ordinary users creating custom agents
  • Opportunity Shift: Professional agent development services and compliance solutions may become new blue ocean markets

2. Market Trend Insights

📊 OPC Model Accelerating Adoption

The intelligent economy is accelerating, and OPC (One Person Company) is rapidly gaining popularity. Leveraging AI agents, no-code tools, and other digital technologies, ordinary entrepreneurs can complete the entire business cycle—from product design to marketing and market entry—without building large teams, using only “personal leadership + AI collaboration.”

⚠️ Risk Warning: Lower technical barriers are attracting many entrepreneurs, leading to issues like homogeneous competition, implementation difficulties, and non-standardized operations. Founders need to establish differentiated advantages to avoid price wars.

🤖 AI-Powered Customer Acquisition Becomes Essential

Xiaohongshu has become a core channel for customer acquisition, but traditional human-intensive approaches are costly, hard to scale, and slow to grow. AI agents for matrix operations are emerging as efficient solutions:

  • One case shows AI agents achieving 3,000+ daily leads
  • Solves three pain points: scattered account management, content inspiration exhaustion, and rising ad costs
  • Suitable for SME owners, physical store owners, and individual entrepreneurs

💼 Corporate Layoffs Parallel AI Investment

In 2026, “AI replacing humans” is shifting from psychological anxiety to reality:

  • Meta cut ~10% of staff in May, redirecting resources to AI
  • Amazon’s latest layoff: 16,000 positions
  • OPC Insight: Corporate cost-cutting creates opportunities for personal services; OPCs can find growth in areas where enterprises are contracting

The critical change in 2026 is AI evolving from “tool attribute” to “decision attribute.” Solo founders are no longer simply using AI for efficiency—they can now leverage AI to participate in and even lead strategic decisions. This means the capability boundaries of individual entrepreneurs are vastly expanded. “One person is a company” is transitioning from vision to reality.
