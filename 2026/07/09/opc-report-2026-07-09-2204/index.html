⚠️ Risk Warning: Cold Reflection Amid the Boom

:::warning
🚨 One-Person Company Boom Fades: 90% of Entrepreneurs Eliminated

2026 was dubbed the “First Year of One-Person Companies.” The popularization of AI tools has drastically lowered the barrier for solo entrepreneurship, with many professionals rushing to register “one-person companies.” However, the boom is rapidly fading, and the brutal reality has emerged: 90% of OPC entrepreneurs have been eliminated.

Core Problems: Who builds the product? Who creates the website? Who connects payments? Who manages data?

The key to truly successful OPC is not “one person doing all the work” but “one person coordinating multiple AI digital employees.”
:::

📋 Startup Recommendations

  1. Verify Before Registering: First validate “chargeable problems” — find problems customers are willing to pay for
  2. Sell Services Before Products: Services are the fastest cash flow and demand validator
  3. First Complete One Small Closed Loop: Includes customer acquisition, transaction, delivery, and repurchase
  4. Choose Vertical Track: AI web series, smartwatches, AI+HR, enterprise intelligent sites, etc.
