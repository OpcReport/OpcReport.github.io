Industry Insights

Essence of OPC: Individual capability through extreme extension via technological leverage. AI tools have evolved from assistant role to programmable, collaborative, core productivity units capable of undertaking systematic tasks.

Fundamental Difference from Freelancing:

  • Freelancers sell personal time and single skills
  • OPC sells systematic delivery capability

Ceiling logic: Freelancing is “how many hours can one person work”, while OPC is “how many agents can you orchestrate to work in parallel”.

⚠️ Entrepreneurship Reminder: The hardest part of OPC isn’t technology. Keys to success: business model validation, user needs mastery, and continuous iteration ability. Technology is just a tool; mindset is everything.
