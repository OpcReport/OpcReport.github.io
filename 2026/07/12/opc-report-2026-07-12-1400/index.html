🔥 Top Stories This Week

1. Policy Dividends Continue, OPC Enters Golden Window

2026 is the best—and possibly the last—wave of OPC (One-Person Company) dividends. The policy foundation is set:

  • New Company Law: Full deregulation—individuals can now establish “one-person companies” and “subsidiary one-person companies”
  • Tax Benefits: Actual tax burden for small/micro enterprises drops to 5% on profits under ¥3M
  • Local Support: Shenzhen Nanshan & Bao’an launch exclusive OPC communities; Shanghai Lingang “Super Individual 288” program
  • National Strategy: 2026 Government Work Report explicitly proposes “Creating New Forms of Intelligent Economy”

2. AI Toolchain Completes Qualitative Change, Agents Become “Digital Employee Matrix”

Today, an OPC entrepreneur can simultaneously deploy multiple digital employees: AI customer service for inquiries, marketing agents for content creation, development agents for product building.

Human business history: The minimum effective entrepreneurial unit has been compressed from “team” to “individual” for the first time.

3. Global OPC Co-Creation Festival Held

At the 2026 Global Digital Economy Conference, the Global OPC (One-Person Company) Co-Creation Festival was held specifically for one-person company entrepreneurs. Zhongguancun AI North Latitude Community has gathered 32 one-person companies, with founders averaging under 30 years old.

💼 Profit Models Map

1️⃣ AI Content Monetization (Lowest Barrier)

Platforms: WeChat Official Account, Short Video, Xiaohongshu, AI Knowledge IP

Revenue streams: Advertising, Commercial Orders, E-commerce, Knowledge Products

AI helps: Topic selection, Writing, Image generation, Editing, Publishing

Income Range: ¥2K-30K/month

2️⃣ AI Operations Agency (Strong Demand)

Helping SMEs with: AI Short Video, AI Customer Acquisition, AI Private Domain, AI Customer Service

Particularly fast growth in: Dining, Beauty, Education, Local Businesses

Income Range: ¥3K-20K/month

3️⃣ AI Agent Building (High-Value Track)

Helping enterprises customize AI workflows, build vertical domain agents for “cost reduction & efficiency improvement”

Income Range: ¥10K-100K+/month

4️⃣ SaaS Product Development (High Ceiling)

Solving real daily pain points with AI → Subscription SaaS products

Case: Pieter Levels maintains 12 products solo, monthly revenue exceeds $300,000 USD

Income Range: ¥30K-1M+/month

5️⃣ AI Freelance Services (Stable Cash Flow)

Using AI tools to improve efficiency, accepting outsourcing for design, development, copywriting, data analysis

Income Range: ¥1.5K-15K/month

6️⃣ Vertical Domain Deep Cultivation (Differentiation)

Finding niche but essential markets, leveraging tech capabilities

Example: AI avatar customization (pet humanization, couple memorial portraits)

Income Range: ¥2K-15K/month

Key: Tech execution is no longer a barrier

Case 4: Lawyer Smart Contract Review System

Key: Traditional industry + AI = New Opportunities

Case 5: AI Avatar Customization - Niche Market

⚠️ Risk Warnings

  1. Lowered barriers increase competition - AI tools make entry easier for everyone
  2. AI output quality varies - Direct AI copying is easily spotted by readers/users
  3. Cash flow management - OPC income can be unstable initially; maintain 3-6 months of living expenses
  4. Compliance risks - AI-generated content requires attention to copyright and privacy regulations
