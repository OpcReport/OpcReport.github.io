🔍 Trend Deep Dive {#trend-analysis}

1. From “Super Individual” to “OPC” Organizational Evolution

OPC is not a “fancy upgrade of self-employed.” Its standard architecture is:

1 core decision-maker + N AI digital employees + ∞ global collaboration network

OPC has an independent legal entity status — entrepreneurs are no longer “odd job workers” but participate in market competition as limited liability companies with accumulable commercial assets and credit systems.

2. “Digital Employee Year”: AI Tool Chain Completes Qualitative Change

AI agents like OpenClaw form a “digital employee matrix”:

  • Smart customer service handles user inquiries
  • Marketing AI agents auto-generate promotional content
  • Development AI agents build products

For the first time in human commercial history, the minimum viable unit for entrepreneurship has compressed from “team” to “individual.”

3. Startup Costs Drop to Historical Lows

One AI agent’s daily task capacity equals 3-5 full-time employees, at less than 1/10 the cost of traditional labor. Using platforms like OpenClaw, entrepreneurs become “super individuals,” independently building commercial-grade applications across vertical domains — AI web series, smartwatches, AI+HR, enterprise smart sites, and more.

4. Three Successful OPC Paths

After analyzing hundreds of OPC cases, only three types are profitable:

  1. Tech experts doing overkill — e.g., Tsinghua PhD student Xu Zhanwei raised 10M+ yuan in 6 months with a smart wristband
  2. Deep niche verticals — specialized in underserved market segments
  3. Service-type OPC by AI efficiency — e.g., Fudan graduate Wang Yifeng reduced article writing from 2-3 hours to under 1 hour with AI, earning above his previous salary in two months

5. OPE Concept: Individual as the “Minimum Innovation System”

Dr. Zhang Qi, Microsoft AI Asia-Pacific President, proposed the “OPE” (One Person Enterprise) concept — under generative AI and deep empowerment, individuals are becoming the “minimum innovation system” capable of orchestrating tools, systems, and global resources.
