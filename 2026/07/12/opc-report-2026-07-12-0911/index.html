🏛️ Policy & Ecosystem Updates

1. China’s First Tencent WorkBuddy OPC Community — Chengdu

Date: July 10, 2026 | Location: Chengdu Hi-Tech Zone, Tianfu Long Island Digital & Cultural Park

China’s first Tencent WorkBuddy OPC Cooperation Community officially launched, jointly initiated by Tencent Cloud and Chengdu Hi-Tech Culture & Media Co., Ltd. The community leverages Tencent’s self-developed AI workbench WorkBuddy, with OPC (One-Person Company) as the core entity, exploring a new multi-party collaborative path of “platform support + community co-creation” for full-process OPC growth. The community launch is also a city-side event of the 2026 APEC Digital Week.

Tags: #Platform #APEC #Chengdu #WorkBuddy

2. Global AI-OPC Ecosystem Community Launched — Beijing

Date: July 9, 2026 | Location: Beijing National Convention Center

The 2026 Digital Economy Industry Expo & Global OPC Co-Creation Festival convened at the Beijing National Convention Center, bringing together OPC entrepreneurs, computing enterprises, platform institutions, and investment organizations from the entire industry chain nationwide. The Global AI-OPC Ecosystem Community was officially released at the event.

Tags: #Ecosystem #Beijing #IndustryChain #GlobalOPC

3. Alibaba Cloud “OPC Innovation Support Plan” — Up to ¥1M Token Subsidy

Alibaba Cloud launched the “OPC Innovation Support Plan,” offering five dimensions of support for 5,000 AI innovation teams: Token subsidies, dedicated technical services, Alibaba ecosystem access, brand support, and financing/startup space connection. The plan includes tiered support with maximum ¥1 million in Token support, one-on-one technical escort, and full lifecycle product services.

Tags: #AlibabaCloud #TokenSubsidy #MillionYuan #Platform

4. Million-Level AI OPC Incubation Project Launched

Date: July 7, 2026 | Organizers: Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Education, MIIT, China Association for Science and Technology

The 2026 National 3D Competition AIGC Multi-modal Innovation Design & Technology Application Competition (AI OPC Industry Track) officially kicked off. Project initiator Lu Taiyu emphasized: “AI is completely reconstructing the underlying logic of entrepreneurship. One-person companies are transitioning from concept to reality.”

Tags: #NationalLevel #AIGCCompetition #Incubation #GovernmentBacked

5. SPD Bank Beijing Branch Launches OPC One-Stop “Toolbox”

At the 2026 Digital Economy Industry Expo, SPD Bank Beijing Branch introduced a one-stop “toolbox” service for OPC entrepreneurs, integrating financial resources and AI tool support to help super-individuals reduce costs and increase efficiency.

Tags: #FinancialSupport #SPDBank #Banking

💼 Notable Cases & Business Insights

🔥 Global Benchmark: Matthew Gallagher Builds $401M Medvi with $20K + AI Agents

Matthew Gallagher, age 41, built Medvi — a healthcare company — in just 2 months with $20,000 startup capital and a dozen AI agents. The company’s underlying code was written by AI, advertising visuals and promotional videos were fully AI-generated, and the 24/7 customer service was handled by AI Agents. In its first year, the company generated $401 million in revenue. 2026 projected sales: $1.8 billion. The company has only 2 full-time employees. In 2025, net profit margin reached 16.2%, approximately $65 million. (For comparison, listed company Hims & Hers had only 5.5% net margin with 2,442 employees.)

Tags: #Medvi #4BillionUSD #SuperIndividual #AIHealthcare

💹 International Data: Loabable & Base44

  • Sweden Loabable: Achieved $100M ARR in 8 months, valuation surged to $6.6 billion
  • Israel Base44: 8-person team (90% code AI-generated) reached $1.5M ARR in 3 weeks, acquired for $80 million

These cases prove that AI OPC has moved from concept to large-scale implementation.

Tags: #Loabable #Unicorn #Acquisition #Base44 #AIStartup

🌍 China Practice: Beijing Future Tide Technology — 5 “AI Employees”

Beijing Future Tide Technology founder Liu Yefei, with 18 years of experience in tech architecture and management at internet companies, now runs his company with 5 “AI employees”: AI handles code writing, code review, cloud server automated deployment, real-time information collection, and investment recommendations. The founder focuses on strategic planning and decision-making. The company launched an AI Digital Employee Engineering Delivery Platform to help SMEs build proprietary digital employees.

Tags: #DigitalEmployee #Beijing #AIDeliveryPlatform

📦 Practical Case: 17 AI Agents Generate ¥5M Annual Revenue

OPCwang.com featured a notable case: an entrepreneur achieved ¥5 million in annual revenue through a carefully configured set of 17 AI Agents collaborating in coordination. The key lies in Agent division of labor — each Agent focuses on specific tasks, connected through workflows to form a complete business closed loop. This embodies the core OPC logic: “Humans make decisions, AI executes.”

Tags: #17Agents #5MRevenue #CollaborationModel #OPC

💰 Main OPC Monetization Paths

Content + Subscription / Digital Courses / Paid Communities

Use AI tools to produce high-quality content at scale, establish paid subscriptions or knowledge communities, achieving “build once, sell many times” passive income.

Vertical SaaS Tools / API Services

Develop SaaS tools or API services for niche scenarios based on AI. Products can auto-deliver, auto-renew. Founders transition from “trading time for money” to “trading systems for money.”

Consulting + Automated Products

With professional expertise as the core and AI tools as the carrier, consulting + product dual drive enables high ticket size and scaling in parallel.

B-End Custom Development

Provide AI digital employee customization and enterprise intelligent transformation services for SMEs — high ticket size, stable demand, one of the most direct OPC monetization paths.

🔭 Lao Qin’s Take: Key Trends This Period

1. Platform + Community Model Becomes Mainstream

Tencent WorkBuddy OPC Community landing in Chengdu, Alibaba Cloud’s million-yuan subsidy plan — these signal that OPC entrepreneurship is transitioning from “individual lone fight” to an ecosystem phase of “platform empowerment + community collaboration.”

2. Global Competition Threshold is Lowering, But Overseas Compliance is the Biggest Challenge

The emergence of Shanghai Lingang’s data cross-border circulation whitelist shows the real barrier to OPC overseas expansion is not technology but compliance. Data cross-border, taxation, and international payments are the three core issues. Whoever solves these problems first gains the first-mover advantage.

3. From “Using AI to Do Things” to “Using AI to Do Business” is the Core Leap

In H1 2026, market focus has shifted from “whether you can use AI tools” to “whether you can convert AI into deliverable products, content, or services.” Whether you can complete this leap determines the life-and-death line of OPC entrepreneurship.

4. Wealth Effect of Super-Individuals is Starting to Show

Cases like Medvi and Loabable are educating the market: one person + AI can create hundred-million-dollar revenue. In H2 2026, more ordinary people are expected to validate this path. OPC wealth stories will shift from “miracle” to “routine.”
