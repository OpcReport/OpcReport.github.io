  1. Digital content & creative services — low barrier, fast cash flow (AI avatar customization, ghostwriting, short-video narration).
  2. AI app / tool development — indie-dev sweet spot; externalize internal tools into SaaS.
  3. Vertical consulting / services — “one meter wide, one hundred meters deep” beats “jack of all trades.”
  4. Cross-border e-commerce / overseas services — let AI handle multilingual support and localization.
  5. AI hardware & vertical applications — the deep-tech talent’s downward strike (e.g., smart wristbands).
  6. Education / knowledge monetization — lightweight auto-closing knowledge-IP model.

Principle: An OPC’s moat is professional depth, not “doing everything.” AI has driven the cost of content, code, and design toward zero — you must own something irreplaceable: industry experience, network, deep insight.
