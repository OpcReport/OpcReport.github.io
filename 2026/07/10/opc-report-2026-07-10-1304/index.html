2. OPC Definition and Core Architecture

OPC (One-Person Company) represents a fundamental transformation in business organization. With AI technology empowerment, individual entrepreneurs can independently operate complete business loops—from product development and marketing to customer service.

Standard OPC Architecture:

Core Characteristics:

  • Independent Legal Entity: Entrepreneurs are no longer “freelancers,” but have buildable business assets and credit systems
  • AI Empowerment: Intelligent agents take over content creation, data analysis, customer communication, and other repetitive tasks
  • Lightweight Operations: Consists of only 1-3 people, each paired with multiple AI agents for collaborative work

7. Pitfall Guide: Core OPC Startup Misconceptions

Common Misconceptions:

  • Misconception 1: Thinking OPC requires AI mastery and mastering N AI Agents
  • Misconception 2: Believing stories of “someone making millions monthly with multiple AI Agents”
  • Misconception 3: Pursuing comprehensive coverage while ignoring niche requirements

Correct Approach:

  • Find a niche requirement that needs to be met
  • Choose tracks small enough that big companies ignore them
  • Provide personalized services that are difficult to industrialize at scale
