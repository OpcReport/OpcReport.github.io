⚠️ Risk Reminders

  • OPC is not risk-free entrepreneurship. Revenue cases follow a long-tail distribution; media amplifies the top samples, so ordinary individuals must prepare cash flow and compliance.
  • AI tools depend on third-party APIs, exposing you to cost volatility, policy and shutdown risk; be wary of “automatic passive income” pitches.
  • A one-person company still carries legal-person liability and tax compliance — confirm address, supervisor and capital-injection requirements before registering.
