🏛️ Policy Updates: Policy Race Heats Up Across China

Beijing Shijingshan Issues OPC Startup Support Policy — Subsidy Cap 6 Million RMB

Date: 2026-07-10

On July 10, 2026, Beijing’s Shijingshan District officially released the “Several Measures for Supporting the Development of OPC Entrepreneurship Ecosystem (Trial Implementation)”, covering 8 major policy categories:

Category Details
Basic Support 100,000 RMB one-time startup fund + 3 months free accommodation for newly registered OPCs
Computing Subsidy 200M RMB computing resource pool for all district OPCs (pool-based allocation)
Commercialization Grant Up to 1M RMB for technology-to-market projects, covering prototype, validation & first-order expansion
Competition Awards Up to 500K RMB one-time reward for OPC projects winning district-level or above competitions
Park Construction Dedicated OPC parks and co-working spaces
Financial Support Financing support and credit enhancement
Housing Security Living support for OPC founders
Government Services Streamlined registration and compliance services

Important Note: The 2 million RMB computing subsidy is a district-wide pool, not a per-company individual quota. Resources are allocated based on actual demand.

Why it matters: Shijingshan is now the third Beijing district (after Haidian and Fengtai) to launch dedicated OPC policies. The policy arms race between Chinese cities for AI solopreneurs is intensifying rapidly.

Source: QQ Penguin Report / 2026-07-10

Tencent WorkBuddy OPC Cooperative Community Officially Launches in Chengdu High-Tech Zone

Date: 2026-07-10

China’s first Tencent WorkBuddy OPC Cooperative Community officially opened at Tianfu Changdao Digital Cultural & Creative Park in Chengdu High-Tech Zone. Jointly initiated by Tencent Cloud and Chengdu Gaoxin Cultural & Creative Media Co., the community leverages Tencent’s self-developed AI work platform WorkBuddy, exploring an “platform support + community co-creation” multi-party collaborative OPC full-process growth model.

Notably, the community’s launch ceremony served as an official APEC Digital Week 2026 city-side event — lending significant international visibility.

Source: QQ Penguin Report / 2026-07-10

National AIGC Innovation Application Competition (AI OPC Track) Kicks Off

Date: 2026-07-03

The “2026 National 3D Competition — AIGC Multimodal Innovation Design & Technology Application Contest (AI OPC Industrial Track)”, jointly guided by China’s Ministry of Science and Technology, Ministry of Education, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, and China Association for Science and Technology, officially launched at a ceremony in Beijing. The million-RMB AI OPC Entrepreneurship Incubation Project also launched simultaneously.

Lu Taiyu, Chairman & CEO of Jinshang Health Group and initiator of the Million AI OPC Incubation Project, emphasized: “AI is completely reconstructing the underlying logic of entrepreneurship — One Person Companies are transitioning from concept to reality.”

Source: QQ Penguin Report / 2026-07-07

OpenHex Showcases Agent Build & Scheduling Platform at Beijing OPC Festival

  • AI Messenger for AI Agents: Multi-agent collaboration, coordinated task execution
  • AI Programmer: Transform ideas into publishable HTML pages instantly — no coding required

📈 Industry Insights: 12 Million+ Individual Entrepreneurs Pour Into OPC Track

Over 12 Million Individual Entrepreneurs Join the OPC Wave in China

Date: 2026

China’s 2026 entrepreneurial landscape is seeing an unprecedented OPC surge. From Beijing Zhongguancun to Wuhan Guanggu to Shanghai Lingang — over 12 million individual entrepreneurs have entered the OPC track nationwide. One person, one computer, one AI system: a complete business loop covering product R&D, content creation, and marketing distribution — all self-operated.

AI tools have shifted from a supporting role to becoming the core productive unit — programmable, collaborative, and capable of handling systematic tasks.

Source: CSDN / 2026-07-08

AI Agent 2026 Technical Architecture Overview

In 2026, AI Agents have evolved from “conversational demos” to core automated execution productivity engines. Key technical components:

  • Perception: Receive instructions and environment/tool feedback
  • Planning/Reasoning: ReAct or Chain-of-Thought for task decomposition
  • Memory: Short-term context + long-term experience accumulation
  • Tool/MCP Calls: Standardized tool scheduling
  • Self-Reflection: Feedback-based error correction and evolution
  • A2A Protocol: Enables cross-platform Agent collaboration like human colleagues

⚠️ Security Alert: ModelScope MS-Agent High-Severity Vulnerability (CVE-2026-2256)

Security researchers discovered a CVSS 9.8 critical command injection vulnerability in the ModelScope MS-Agent framework (v1.6.0rc1 and earlier). Attackers can craft prompt injection attacks to trick the AI Agent into executing malicious OS commands, potentially achieving full system compromise.

Recommendations for OPC founders:

  • Immediately update to the latest framework version
  • Avoid processing untrusted documents or user-provided prompts without sanitization
  • Monitor official security advisories for your AI tooling stack

Source: CSDN / 2026-06-06
