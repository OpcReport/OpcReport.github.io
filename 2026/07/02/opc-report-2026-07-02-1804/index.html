4.2 Agility Advantage

  • R&D cycle compressed from months to days
  • One-person leadership + AI execution, avoiding team communication costs
  • Light asset operation, reducing entrepreneurial risk

4.3 Technology Leverage

  • AI tools cover full business chain: product design, R&D, marketing, customer service, finance
  • Open-source models + commercial API combination, flexible cost control
  • Serverless architecture, pay-by-actual-usage
  • Global payment gateway, product can receive payments from global users on the first day of launch
