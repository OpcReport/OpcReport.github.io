5.2 Workflow Reconstruction: OPC’s Core Competitiveness

The true core of OPC is not “replacing humans with AI in traditional workflows”, but reconstructing workflows based on AI capability models.

⚠️ Complexity of Traditional Workflows = Intrinsic Complexity of Things + Human Limitation Compensation Layer

  • Human brain capacity limited → Split into segments
  • Human collaboration requires documentation → Intermediate products
  • Human collaboration has losses → Management and coordination

These complexities are only related to human limitations, not to the things themselves. OPC removes these compensation layers through AI, achieving minimalist workflows.
